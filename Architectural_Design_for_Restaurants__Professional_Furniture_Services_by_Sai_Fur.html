Common Furniture Problems in Surat Restaurants

Restaurants in Surat face unique challenges. The humid climate and fluctuating temperatures can significantly impact the lifespan and quality of furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and warping: Humidity causes wood to expand and contract, leading to damage. Fabric fading and staining: Sunlight and spills can quickly degrade upholstery. Metal rust and corrosion: The humid climate accelerates the rusting process. Pest infestation: Termites and other pests can damage furniture.

These problems not only impact the aesthetic appeal of your restaurant but also lead to costly repairs or replacements. Ignoring these issues can also create safety hazards for your staff and customers.

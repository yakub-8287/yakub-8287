The Challenges of Furniture in Surat's Climate

Surat's unique climate, characterized by high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, poses significant challenges to your furniture. The intense heat and dampness can lead to:

Wood warping and cracking: The wood expands and contracts, leading to unsightly cracks and damage. Weakened joints and frames: Humidity weakens the glue holding your furniture together. Fading and discoloration of upholstery and finishes: Sunlight and humidity can dramatically change the look of your furniture over time. Pest infestation: The warm, humid climate can attract pests like termites and wood borers, compromising the structural integrity of your furniture.

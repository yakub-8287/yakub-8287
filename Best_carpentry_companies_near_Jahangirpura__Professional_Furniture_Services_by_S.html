Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's climate, with its fluctuating humidity and temperatures, can significantly affect your furniture. Common problems include:

Wood warping and cracking: Changes in humidity can cause wood to expand and contract, leading to cracks and warping, especially in older pieces. Pest infestation: Warm, humid conditions can attract wood-boring insects, damaging the structural integrity of your furniture. Fading and discoloration: Sun exposure can cause furniture to fade and lose its vibrant color, especially in homes with large windows facing south or west. General wear and tear: Daily use inevitably leads to scratches, dents, and loose joints, affecting both the functionality and aesthetic appeal of your furniture.

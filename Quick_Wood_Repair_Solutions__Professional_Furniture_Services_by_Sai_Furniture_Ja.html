The Problem: Damaged Furniture in Surat's Climate

Surat's unique climate, characterized by high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, can wreak havoc on wooden furniture. The intense heat and moisture can lead to warping, cracking, and even pest infestations. These issues not only affect the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but can also pose safety risks, especially if structural integrity is compromised. Ignoring these problems can lead to costly replacements down the line. The beautiful, intricately carved teakwood furniture that is common in many Surat homes is particularly susceptible to climate damage.

Common Furniture Problems in Surat: The Challenges of Our Climate

Surat's climate, with its fluctuating humidity and temperatures, presents unique challenges to furniture. Common problems include:

Wood rot and warping: High humidity weakens wood, leading to rotting and warping, especially in older, untreated pieces. Cracked and chipped surfaces: Temperature changes can cause furniture surfaces to crack, chip, or peel. Loose joints and instability: Years of use can loosen joints, making furniture unstable and unsafe. Pest infestation: Certain insects are attracted to wooden furniture, causing damage and potentially compromising its structural integrity. Upholstery damage: Stains, tears, and fading are common problems with fabric upholstery, particularly in high-traffic areas.

These issues not only affect the aesthetics of your furniture but can also pose safety risks, especially for older pieces. Ignoring these problems can lead to more extensive and costly repairs in the future.

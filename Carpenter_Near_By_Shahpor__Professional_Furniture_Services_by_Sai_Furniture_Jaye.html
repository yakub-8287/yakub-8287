Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat, with its humid climate and fluctuating temperatures, presents unique challenges for furniture. Common problems include:

Wood rot and decay: The humidity can cause wood to swell, warp, and even rot, damaging the structural integrity of your furniture. Pest infestation: Termites and other wood-boring insects thrive in humid environments, potentially causing significant damage to your valuable pieces. Cracks and splits: Temperature fluctuations can lead to cracks and splits in wooden furniture, especially older pieces. Loose joints and broken components: Daily wear and tear can loosen joints and break components, requiring professional repair.

These issues not only affect the aesthetics of your furniture but can also pose safety risks. Ignoring these problems can lead to costly repairs or even complete replacement down the line.

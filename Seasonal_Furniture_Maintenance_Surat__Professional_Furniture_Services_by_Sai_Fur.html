Common Furniture Problems in Surat's Climate

Surat's climate, characterized by high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, can wreak havoc on your furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and warping: Excess moisture leads to wood deterioration and warping, especially in wooden furniture. Polishing damage: Extreme temperature fluctuations can cause cracking and peeling of furniture polish. Pest infestation: Humidity provides a breeding ground for insects that can damage wooden structures and upholstery. Fabric fading: Direct sunlight can cause fading and discoloration of fabrics. Metal rust: Exposure to moisture can cause rusting on metallic components.

These problems not only compromise the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but also significantly impact its longevity and potentially pose safety risks. Neglecting timely maintenance can lead to expensive repairs or even complete replacement down the line.

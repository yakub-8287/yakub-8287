The Challenges of Furniture in Surat's Climate

Surat, with its humid climate and fluctuating temperatures, presents unique challenges for furniture. Common issues include:

Wood warping and cracking: The humidity can cause wood to expand and contract, leading to damage. Pest infestation: Certain insects thrive in humid conditions, damaging furniture. Fading and discoloration: Sunlight can cause furniture to fade over time, especially in areas with ample sunlight exposure near the Tapi River. Wear and tear: Daily use inevitably leads to wear and tear, particularly on high-traffic pieces.

These issues can lead to costly repairs or even the need to replace your furniture prematurely. Choosing the right carpenter is therefore crucial for both longevity and cost-effectiveness.

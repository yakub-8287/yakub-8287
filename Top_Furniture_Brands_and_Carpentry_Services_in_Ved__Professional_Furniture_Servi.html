Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's climate presents unique challenges to furniture. High humidity can lead to wood warping, swelling, and mildew, while fluctuating temperatures cause expansion and contraction, stressing joints and finishes. Termites and other pests are also common issues, potentially causing significant structural damage. These problems can lead to expensive repairs or even complete furniture replacement. Ignoring these issues can also pose safety risks, such as wobbly chairs or broken tables.

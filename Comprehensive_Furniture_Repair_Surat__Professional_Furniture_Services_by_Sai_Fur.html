Common Furniture Problems in Surat: The Challenges of Gujarat's Climate

Surat's climate, with its fluctuating humidity and temperature, presents unique challenges for furniture. Common problems include:

Wood rot and warping: The high humidity can cause wood to swell, crack, and even rot. Mildew and mold: Dampness encourages the growth of harmful fungi, damaging both the wood and upholstery. Insect infestations: Certain insects thrive in warm, humid environments, causing significant damage to wooden furniture. Upholstery wear and tear: Regular use, along with humidity, can lead to fabric fading, tearing, and general deterioration.

These problems not only affect the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but also pose potential safety hazards. Ignoring these issues can lead to expensive replacements, rather than a simple and cost-effective repair.

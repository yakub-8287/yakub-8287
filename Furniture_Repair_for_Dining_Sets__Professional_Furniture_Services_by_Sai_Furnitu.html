2. Common Dining Set Problems in Surat's Climate

Surat's unique climate, with its fluctuating humidity and temperatures, can significantly impact your furniture. Common problems include:

Wood warping or cracking: Changes in humidity can cause wood to expand and contract, leading to cracks and warping. Loose joints and wobbly legs: Years of use and environmental changes weaken joints, resulting in instability. Damaged veneer: Veneer can peel or become scratched, diminishing the aesthetic appeal of your dining set. Pest infestation: Certain insects thrive in humid climates and can damage wooden furniture.

Ignoring these issues can lead to further damage, increasing repair costs and even posing safety risks.

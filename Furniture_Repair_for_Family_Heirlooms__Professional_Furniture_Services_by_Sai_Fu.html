Common Furniture Problems in Surat and Their Solutions

Surat's climate, with its fluctuating humidity and temperatures, can take a toll on your furniture. Common issues we encounter include:

Wood rot and decay: The humid Surat summers can cause wood to rot and weaken, leading to structural instability. Cracked or damaged veneers: Changes in temperature can cause veneers to crack and peel. Loose joints and broken legs: Years of use can loosen joints and cause legs to break. Faded finishes: Sunlight and dust can cause finishes to fade and dull. Insect infestation: Wood-boring insects can cause significant damage if left untreated.

These problems not only diminish the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but also pose safety risks. Ignoring these issues can lead to costly repairs down the line and, in some cases, irreparable damage. Addressing these problems promptly is crucial for preserving your valuable heirlooms.

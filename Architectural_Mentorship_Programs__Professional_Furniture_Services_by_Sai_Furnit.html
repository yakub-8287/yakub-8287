2. The Problem: Surat's Furniture Challenges

Surat's humid climate and fluctuating temperatures pose significant threats to furniture. Exposure to moisture can lead to warping, cracking, and the growth of mold and mildew, especially in traditional wooden furniture prevalent in the region. Ignoring these issues can lead to costly repairs down the line, or even irreversible damage. Furthermore, improper repairs can compromise the structural integrity and pose safety risks. The high cost of replacing damaged furniture is another significant concern.

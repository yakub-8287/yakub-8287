Common Furniture Problems in Surat: The Challenges of Heat and Humidity

Surat's climate, characterized by high humidity and temperature fluctuations, poses significant challenges to furniture. Common problems include:

Wood rot and warping: Excessive moisture can cause wood to swell, crack, and rot, particularly in older, untreated furniture. Insect infestation: Warm, humid conditions create an ideal breeding ground for wood-boring insects, damaging the structure and finish of your furniture. Fading and discoloration: Sunlight and humidity can cause furniture finishes to fade and become dull over time. Upholstery damage: Wear and tear, spills, and humidity can affect upholstery fabrics.

Ignoring these issues can lead to costly repairs down the line and even potential safety hazards.

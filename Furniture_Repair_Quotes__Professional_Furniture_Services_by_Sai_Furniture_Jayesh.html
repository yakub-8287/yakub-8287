Common Furniture Problems in Surat & Their Solutions

Surat's climate, with its high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, can significantly impact your furniture's condition. Common problems include:

Wood rot and warping: The humid climate can cause wood to absorb moisture, leading to warping, cracking, and even rot. Upholstery damage: Exposure to dust and sun can fade and damage upholstery fabrics. Loose joints and broken parts: Regular use can loosen joints and cause damage to legs, frames, and other components. Pest infestations: Termites and other insects can infest wooden furniture, causing significant damage.

Ignoring these issues can lead to costly replacements and safety hazards. Our expert assessment helps identify problems early and offers cost-effective solutions.

The Challenges Faced by Antique Furniture in Surat

Surat's climate, with its fluctuating humidity and temperatures, presents unique challenges for antique furniture. Common issues we encounter include:

Woodworm infestation: The warm, humid climate can attract wood-boring insects, causing significant damage. Cracked or warped wood: Seasonal changes in temperature and humidity can lead to wood expansion and contraction, causing cracks and warping. Faded or damaged finishes: Exposure to sunlight and dust can dull or damage the original finish of your antique furniture. Loose joints and broken components: Years of use can weaken joints and lead to broken legs, drawers, or other parts.

Ignoring these problems can lead to further deterioration, escalating repair costs and, potentially, irreparable damage.

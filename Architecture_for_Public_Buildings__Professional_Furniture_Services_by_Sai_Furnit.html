Common Furniture Issues in Surat's Public Spaces

Surat's unique climate, characterized by high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, poses significant challenges to the longevity of public building furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and warping: The humid environment encourages fungal growth, leading to structural damage. Fabric fading and deterioration: Exposure to sunlight and heat can severely impact the lifespan of upholstery. Metal corrosion: Rust and oxidation are common issues, especially in outdoor furniture. Wear and tear: High foot traffic in public spaces inevitably leads to damage and breakage.

These issues not only affect the aesthetics of your building but also pose safety risks and incur significant repair costs in the long run.

2. Common Furniture Problems in Surat: Understanding the Challenges

Surat's humid climate and fluctuating temperatures pose significant challenges for wooden furniture. Common issues include:

Pest infestation: Termites and woodworms are a constant threat, weakening the structure of your furniture.

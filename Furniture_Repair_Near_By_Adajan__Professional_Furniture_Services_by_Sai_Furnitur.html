Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's hot and humid climate presents unique challenges for your furniture. The intense heat and humidity can lead to:

Pest infestations: Warm temperatures can attract wood-boring insects, leading to extensive damage. Loose joints and structural issues: Years of wear and tear, especially on heavily used pieces, can cause joints to loosen and structural problems to arise.

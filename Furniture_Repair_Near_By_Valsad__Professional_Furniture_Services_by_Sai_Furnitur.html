Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's climate, with its high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, can significantly impact your furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and warping: The humid conditions can cause wood to absorb moisture, leading to rotting and warping. Cracked or chipped finishes: Temperature changes can cause finishes to crack or chip, especially in older pieces. Loose joints and broken legs: Years of use can weaken joints and cause legs to break. Upholstery damage: Exposure to sun and dust can fade and damage upholstery fabrics. Pest infestations: Certain insects and pests are drawn to older wooden furniture, potentially causing significant damage.

These problems not only affect the aesthetics of your furniture but can also pose safety risks. Ignoring these issues can lead to more extensive and costly repairs in the future.

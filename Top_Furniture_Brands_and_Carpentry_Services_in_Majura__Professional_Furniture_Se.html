Common Furniture Problems in Surat

The humid climate of Surat, Gujarat, coupled with fluctuating temperatures, presents unique challenges for furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and warping: Excessive moisture can damage wooden furniture, leading to cracking and decay. Pest infestation: Warm, humid conditions create a breeding ground for termites and other wood-boring insects. Fabric damage: Sun exposure and humidity can fade colors and weaken fabric on upholstered pieces. Joint failure: Years of use and climate exposure can weaken the joints of furniture, resulting in instability.

Ignoring these problems can lead to costly repairs down the line, compromising both the aesthetics and safety of your furniture.

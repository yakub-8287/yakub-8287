Common Furniture Problems in Surat & Their Solutions

Surat's climate, characterized by high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, presents unique challenges for furniture. Common problems include:

Wood rot and warping: Excess moisture can cause wood to swell, crack, and even rot, particularly in traditionally crafted pieces. Fading and discoloration: Prolonged sun exposure can fade fabrics and finishes, especially in homes with large windows facing east or west, a common sight in many Surat homes. Pest infestation: Certain insects thrive in humid conditions, leading to damage to furniture structures and upholstery. Loose joints and broken components: Regular use can cause wear and tear, resulting in loose joints, broken legs, or damaged drawers. Upholstery damage: Spills, stains, and general wear and tear can quickly degrade upholstery fabric. Maintaining your furnishings prevents costly replacements.

Ignoring these problems can lead to expensive repairs or even the need to replace your furniture entirely. Regular maintenance is key to preventing significant damage and maintaining the value of your investment.

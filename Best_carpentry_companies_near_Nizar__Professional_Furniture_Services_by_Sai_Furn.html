The Furniture Problems Faced in Surat

Surat, with its vibrant culture and humid climate, presents unique challenges for furniture. Common problems include:

Wood rot and warping: The high humidity can cause wood to swell, crack, and rot, especially in older pieces. Pest infestations: Termites and other wood-boring insects are a common concern in Surat, damaging furniture structures. Sun damage: Intense sunlight can fade and crack furniture finishes, diminishing its aesthetic appeal. General wear and tear: Daily use takes its toll, resulting in loose joints, scratched surfaces, and broken components.

These issues not only affect the look of your furniture but can also pose safety risks and lead to costly replacements.

Common Furniture Problems in Surat & Their Solutions

Surat's unique climate, characterized by high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, significantly impacts furniture. Common problems include:

Wood rot and warping: Excessive moisture can cause wood to swell, crack, and rot, especially in older pieces. Loose joints and broken components: Regular use and temperature changes can weaken joints, leading to instability. Pest infestations: Termites and other pests thrive in humid conditions, damaging wooden structures. Faded finishes and scratches: Sunlight and daily wear and tear contribute to the deterioration of furniture finishes.

These issues not only compromise the aesthetics of your furniture but can also pose safety risks. Ignoring these problems can lead to costly replacements down the line. Sai Furniture (Jayeshbhai) offers affordable solutions to preserve your furniture’s beauty and longevity.

2. Problem Statement: Common Furniture Issues in Surat's Commercial Spaces

Surat's unique climate, characterized by high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, can significantly impact the lifespan of commercial furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and warping: The humid Surat climate can cause wood to absorb moisture, leading to rot, warping, and structural damage. Fabric wear and tear: High traffic in commercial spaces accelerates fabric wear, especially in high-use areas like waiting rooms or lobbies. Metal corrosion: Exposure to humidity and dust can cause rust and corrosion on metal furniture frames. Upholstery stains and tears: Spills and everyday wear and tear can leave upholstery looking unsightly.

These problems not only impact the aesthetic appeal of your space but also carry safety and financial implications. Replacing damaged furniture can be a significant expense, impacting your bottom line.

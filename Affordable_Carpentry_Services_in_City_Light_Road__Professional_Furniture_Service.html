Common Furniture Problems in Surat

The hot and humid climate of Surat can significantly impact your furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and warping: High humidity causes wood to absorb moisture, leading to rot, swelling, and warping. Pest infestation: Warm temperatures create ideal breeding grounds for termites and other wood-boring insects. Sun damage: Direct sunlight can fade finishes and crack the wood. Wear and tear: Daily use can lead to scratches, dents, and broken components.

These problems not only affect the aesthetics of your furniture but can also pose safety hazards and incur significant repair costs.

Surat's hot and humid climate can take its toll on furniture. Common issues include:

Pest infestations: Termites and other pests are a significant concern in Surat, potentially causing significant damage. Accidental damage: Everyday bumps and scratches are inevitable, leading to chipped surfaces and broken components.

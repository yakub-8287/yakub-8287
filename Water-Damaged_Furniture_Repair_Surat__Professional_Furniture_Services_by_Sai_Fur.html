Surat's climate, with its fluctuating humidity and temperatures, poses significant risks to your furniture. Unexpected leaks, monsoon flooding, or even simple spills can lead to:

Mold and mildew growth: Damaging the structural integrity and creating health hazards. Warping and cracking: Particularly in wooden pieces. Discoloration and staining: Affecting the aesthetic appeal of your furniture. Upholstery damage: Causing musty odors and potential pest infestations.

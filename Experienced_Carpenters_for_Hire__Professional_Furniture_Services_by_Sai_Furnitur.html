Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's humid climate and fluctuating temperatures can wreak havoc on your furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and warping: Excessive moisture can cause wood to rot, leading to structural damage and instability. Pest infestations: Certain insects thrive in humid environments, damaging the wood and its integrity. Loose joints and broken components: Years of use can loosen joints, resulting in wobbly tables or broken chairs. Damaged finishes: Extreme temperature changes can cause paint or varnish to crack and peel. These problems not only affect the aesthetics of your furniture but also impact its functionality and lifespan, leading to costly replacements. Ignoring these issues can also cause safety hazards.

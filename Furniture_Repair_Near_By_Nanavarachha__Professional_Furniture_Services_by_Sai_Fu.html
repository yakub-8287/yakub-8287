Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's climate, with its fluctuating humidity and temperatures, presents unique challenges to furniture. Common issues we encounter include:

Wood rot and warping: The humidity can cause wood to expand and contract, leading to warping, cracking, and even rot. Upholstery damage: Sunlight and dust can fade and damage upholstery, while spills and stains can leave permanent marks. Loose joints and broken components: Years of use can loosen joints and cause components to break, impacting both the functionality and aesthetics of your furniture. Pest infestation: Certain insects can infest wooden furniture, causing significant damage if left untreated. Scratches and scuffs: Daily wear and tear can accumulate, leaving unsightly marks on your furniture's surface.

These problems not only detract from the appearance of your furniture but can also pose safety risks and lead to costly replacements.

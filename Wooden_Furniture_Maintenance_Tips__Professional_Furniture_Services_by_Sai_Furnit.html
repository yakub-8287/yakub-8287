Common Furniture Problems in Surat: The Challenges of Our Climate

Surat's humid climate and fluctuating temperatures can wreak havoc on wooden furniture. Common issues include:

Warping and cracking: Changes in humidity cause wood to expand and contract, leading to damage. Fading and discoloration: Sun exposure can cause the wood to fade and lose its vibrancy. Pest infestation: Wood-boring insects are a significant concern in humid environments. Scratches and dents: Daily wear and tear take a toll on the finish. Loose joints and instability: Over time, furniture joints can become loose, making the piece unsafe.

Ignoring these problems can lead to costly repairs or even the need for replacement, making proper maintenance crucial.

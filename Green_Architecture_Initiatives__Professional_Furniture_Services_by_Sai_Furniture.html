The Challenges Facing Furniture in Surat

Surat's unique climate presents several challenges for furniture. The high humidity and fluctuating temperatures can lead to:

Wood warping and cracking: Especially prevalent in older, untreated pieces common in many Surat homes. Pest infestations: Warm, humid conditions create ideal breeding grounds for insects damaging your furniture. Fabric fading and deterioration: Intense sunlight can quickly fade fabrics, reducing the lifespan and aesthetic appeal of your furniture. High repair costs: Neglecting these issues can result in costly replacements rather than simple repairs.

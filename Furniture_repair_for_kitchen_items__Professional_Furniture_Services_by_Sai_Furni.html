Common Kitchen Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's humid climate and fluctuating temperatures can significantly impact kitchen furniture. Common problems include:

Warping and Swelling: Moisture damage leads to warping and swelling of wood, especially in cabinets and chairs. Cracks and Splits: Changes in temperature can cause wood to crack or split, compromising both aesthetics and structural integrity. Peeling Veneer and Paint: The high humidity can lead to paint peeling and veneer lifting. Loose Joints and Hardware: Years of use can loosen joints and hardware, causing instability and safety concerns. Pest Infestation: Some wooden furniture is susceptible to termite or other pest damage.

These issues not only affect the appearance of your kitchen but also impact its functionality and safety, leading to costly replacements.

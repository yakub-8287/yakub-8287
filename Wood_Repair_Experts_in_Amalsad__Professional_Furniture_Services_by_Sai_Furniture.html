Common Furniture Problems in Surat & Amalsad

The climate in Surat, Gujarat presents unique challenges for wooden furniture. High humidity can lead to wood rot, warping, and the growth of mold and mildew. Extreme temperatures cause cracking and splitting, particularly in older or poorly maintained pieces. These issues not only detract from the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but can also compromise its structural integrity, leading to safety concerns and costly replacements. Furthermore, attempting DIY repairs often leads to further damage, adding to the overall cost. Ignoring these problems can quickly turn a small issue into a major expense. For example, a simple scratch left untreated can lead to extensive wood decay over time.

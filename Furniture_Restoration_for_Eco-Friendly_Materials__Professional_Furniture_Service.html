2. Problem Statement: Common Furniture Challenges in Surat

Surat's climate presents unique challenges to furniture. The high humidity and fluctuating temperatures can cause warping, cracking, and fading of wood, particularly in traditional Gujarati furniture styles. These issues not only diminish the aesthetic appeal but also compromise the structural integrity and safety of your pieces. Repairing damaged furniture can be expensive, and using non-eco-friendly materials can add to the environmental burden. Furthermore, improper repairs can lead to safety hazards, especially with antique or structurally compromised pieces.

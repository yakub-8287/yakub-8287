Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's climate, characterized by high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, can take a toll on your furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and damage: Excess moisture can lead to warping, cracking, and rotting, especially in wooden furniture. Upholstery wear and tear: The humid climate can affect fabric and foam, leading to discoloration, sagging, and damage. Pest infestation: Certain wood-boring insects thrive in humid conditions, causing significant damage. Accidental damage: Scratches, dents, and broken parts are common occurrences in busy households.

Ignoring these issues not only compromises the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but also presents safety concerns and can lead to costly replacements down the line.

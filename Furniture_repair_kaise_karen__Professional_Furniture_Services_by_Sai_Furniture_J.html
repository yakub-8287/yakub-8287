Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's humid climate and fluctuating temperatures pose significant challenges to furniture. Common problems include:

Wood rot and warping: Excess moisture can cause wood to expand, crack, and even rot. Loose joints and broken legs: Frequent use and temperature changes can weaken joints. Upholstery damage: Tears, stains, and fading are common issues, particularly with exposed fabrics. Insect infestation: Certain wood-boring insects thrive in humid environments. Scratching and damage: Daily wear and tear can lead to minor to major scratches.

These issues can not only impact the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but also create safety hazards. Repairing them promptly is crucial to prolong the lifespan of your furniture and to keep your family safe.

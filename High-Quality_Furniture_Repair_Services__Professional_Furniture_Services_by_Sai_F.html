Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's hot and humid climate presents unique challenges for furniture. The fluctuating temperatures and high humidity can cause wood to warp, crack, and become susceptible to pest infestations. Common problems we address include:

Wood rot and decay: Particularly prevalent in older furniture exposed to moisture. Cracked and damaged wood: Resulting from temperature fluctuations and general wear and tear. Loose joints and broken parts: Requiring professional repair to maintain structural integrity. Upholstery damage: Tears, stains, and fading common in high-traffic areas. Pest infestations: Termites and wood-boring beetles can significantly damage furniture.

Ignoring these issues can lead to costly replacements and compromise the safety of your furniture.

Common Furniture Problems in Surat: Understanding the Challenges

Surat's climate, with its fluctuating humidity and temperatures, puts significant stress on wooden furniture. Common issues we encounter include:

Wood warping and cracking: Due to fluctuating humidity. Loose joints and weakened structures: Caused by expansion and contraction of wood. Pest infestation: A problem exacerbated by the warm, humid climate. Faded finishes: Sun exposure and humidity can dull the vibrancy of wood finishes.

These problems not only affect the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but can also compromise its structural integrity, leading to safety concerns and costly replacements.

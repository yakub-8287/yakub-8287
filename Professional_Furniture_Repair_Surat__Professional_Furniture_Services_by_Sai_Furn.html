2. Problem Statement: Common Furniture Issues in Surat

Surat's hot and humid climate presents unique challenges for furniture. Common problems include:

Wood rot and warping: High humidity leads to wood expanding and contracting, causing warping, cracking, and even rot, particularly affecting traditional Gujarati furniture pieces. Pest infestation: Warm temperatures create ideal breeding grounds for wood-boring insects, damaging furniture structures. Upholstery damage: Heat and humidity can cause fabrics to fade, stretch, and become damaged. Loose joints and broken parts: Years of use and the climate's effects can weaken joints and lead to breakage. Costly replacements: Replacing damaged furniture can be expensive, especially for antique or custom-made pieces.

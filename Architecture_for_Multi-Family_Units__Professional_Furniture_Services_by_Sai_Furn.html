Common Furniture Problems in Surat's Multi-Family Units

Surat's climate, characterized by high humidity and temperature variations, significantly impacts furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and warping: The humidity can cause wood furniture to swell, crack, and even rot, especially in poorly ventilated areas. Pest infestation: The warm, humid conditions are conducive to termite and other pest infestations, damaging structural integrity and furniture alike. Fabric fading and deterioration: Sunlight and humidity can cause fabric upholstery to fade, become brittle, and lose its original quality. Metal corrosion: The humidity can accelerate the corrosion process in metal furniture frames.

These issues not only detract from the aesthetic appeal of your property but also incur significant repair costs and potentially compromise the safety and comfort of your tenants.

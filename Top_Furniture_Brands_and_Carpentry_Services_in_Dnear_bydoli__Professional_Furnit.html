Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's unique climate, with its high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, can significantly impact your furniture. Common problems include:

Wood warping and cracking: Changes in temperature and humidity can cause wood to expand and contract, leading to warping, cracking, and splitting. Pest infestations: The warm, humid climate can attract wood-boring insects, causing damage and weakening furniture structures. Upholstery wear and tear: Consistent use and exposure to dust and sunlight can cause upholstery to fade, tear, and become uncomfortable. Loose joints and broken components: Over time, even the sturdiest furniture can suffer from loose joints, broken legs, or damaged drawers.

These issues not only affect the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but can also pose safety risks, especially if children are involved. Ignoring these problems can lead to costly repairs down the line.

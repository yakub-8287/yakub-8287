The Challenges Faced by Surat Furniture

Surat's climate, with its fluctuating humidity and temperatures, presents significant challenges for furniture. Excessive moisture can lead to warping, cracking, and the growth of mold and mildew, while high temperatures can cause wood to dry out and become brittle. These issues lead to costly repairs or even the need for complete furniture replacement. Common problems include damaged joints, peeling veneers, scratches, and insect infestations. Ignoring these issues can not only diminish the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but also pose safety risks.

Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's humid climate and fluctuating temperatures can wreak havoc on your furniture. From warping wood to peeling finishes, these environmental factors often lead to costly repairs or even complete replacements. Common issues we encounter include:

Wood rot and decay: High humidity can cause wood to absorb excessive moisture, leading to rot and structural damage. Cracked and split wood: Temperature fluctuations cause wood to expand and contract, resulting in unsightly cracks and splits. Loose joints and broken pieces: Years of use, coupled with environmental stress, often loosen joints and cause furniture to break. Pest infestations: Warm and humid conditions can attract insects like termites, leading to extensive damage if left untreated.

These problems not only impact the aesthetics of your furniture but can also pose safety risks. Ignoring these issues can lead to more significant and expensive repairs in the long run.

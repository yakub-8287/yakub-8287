Common Furniture Problems in Surat & Their Solutions

Surat, with its vibrant culture and unique climate, presents specific challenges for furniture. The high humidity and fluctuating temperatures can lead to several common issues:

Wood rot and damage: Moisture weakens wood, leading to warping, cracking, and even rot. This is particularly prevalent in older furniture. Loose joints and broken pieces: Years of use and shifting temperatures can cause joints to loosen or break completely. Upholstery wear and tear: Sunlight and regular use can fade colors and damage fabrics on sofas, chairs, and other upholstered furniture. Insect infestation: Certain insects are attracted to wood, causing damage that weakens furniture's structure.

Ignoring these problems can lead to costly replacements down the line. Furthermore, damaged furniture can pose safety hazards, especially for children. Sai Furniture provides affordable solutions to these common issues.

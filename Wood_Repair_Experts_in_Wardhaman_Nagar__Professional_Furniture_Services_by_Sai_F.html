Common Furniture Problems in Surat: The Challenges of Gujarat's Climate

Surat's unique climate, with its fluctuating humidity and temperatures, often takes a toll on wooden furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and decay: High humidity can lead to fungal growth and weakening of wood structures. Cracks and splits: Temperature changes cause wood to expand and contract, resulting in cracks. Scratches and dents: Daily wear and tear inevitably leaves marks on your furniture. Loose joints and broken components: Over time, stress can loosen joints or cause complete breakage. Pest infestation: Termites and other insects can damage the integrity of wooden pieces.

These problems not only affect the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but can also compromise its safety and structural integrity, leading to significant costs for replacement. Ignoring these issues can lead to more extensive and costly repairs down the line.

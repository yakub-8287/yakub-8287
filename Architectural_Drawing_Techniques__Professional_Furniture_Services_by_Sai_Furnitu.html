The Challenges of Furniture in Surat's Climate

Surat's unique climate presents several challenges for furniture. The high humidity and fluctuating temperatures can cause warping, cracking, and pest infestations. Common problems we encounter include:

Wood rot and decay: Due to prolonged exposure to moisture. Cracked joints and frames: Caused by temperature changes and age. Insect damage: Termites and other pests can weaken structures unnoticed. Fading and discoloration: Sunlight can significantly affect the finish.

These issues not only diminish the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but also pose safety risks and can lead to costly replacements.

Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's climate, with its fluctuating humidity and temperatures, can significantly impact furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and warping: The humidity can cause wood to expand and contract, leading to warping, cracking, and even rot. Pest infestation: Warm weather can attract termites and other wood-boring insects, damaging furniture structures. Sun damage: Direct sunlight can fade finishes and weaken wood, causing discoloration and cracking. General wear and tear: Everyday use can cause scratches, dents, and loose joints.

These issues not only affect the aesthetics of your furniture but can also compromise its structural integrity, leading to costly repairs down the line. Ignoring these problems can result in irreparable damage, necessitating replacement and considerable expense. Safety can also be a concern, with weakened structures posing a risk of injury.

The Furniture Problems Faced in Surat

Surat's hot and humid climate presents unique challenges for furniture. The constant fluctuations in temperature and humidity can cause wood to warp, crack, and deteriorate. This is especially true for older pieces or those made from less durable materials. Common problems include:

Cracked or damaged wood: Extreme temperatures can cause wood to expand and contract, leading to cracks and splits. Loose joints and wobbly legs: Humidity can weaken the glue used in furniture construction. Pest infestation: Warm and humid conditions can attract termites and other wood-boring insects. Faded or peeling finishes: Sunlight and humidity can damage the finish on your furniture.

Ignoring these problems can lead to more costly repairs in the future and potentially compromise the safety of your furniture.

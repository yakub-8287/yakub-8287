Common Furniture Problems in Surat: The Challenges of Gujarat's Climate

Surat's humid climate and fluctuating temperatures can wreak havoc on your furniture. Common issues we encounter include:

Wood rot and warping: The high humidity can cause wood to expand and contract, leading to cracking and warping. Fabric damage: Moisture and mildew can stain and weaken upholstery fabrics. Loose joints and broken legs: Regular wear and tear, exacerbated by humidity, can loosen joints and break chair legs. Pest infestations: Warm, humid conditions can attract pests that damage wood and upholstery.

These problems not only affect the aesthetics of your furniture but can also compromise its safety and structural integrity, potentially leading to costly replacements.

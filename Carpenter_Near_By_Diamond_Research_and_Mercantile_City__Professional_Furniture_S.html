Common Furniture Problems in Surat & Their Solutions

Surat's unique climate, characterized by high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, can significantly impact the condition of your furniture. Common problems include:

Wood warping and cracking: The fluctuating humidity levels can cause wood to expand and contract, leading to warping, cracks, and splits. Pest infestation: Warm, humid conditions create ideal breeding grounds for wood-boring insects. Damaged upholstery: Moisture can damage upholstery fabric, causing discoloration, mildew, and structural weakening. Loose joints and broken hardware: Years of use can take their toll, leading to loose joints, broken drawers, and damaged hinges.

These problems can not only detract from the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but also pose safety risks. Ignoring these issues can lead to costly repairs down the line.

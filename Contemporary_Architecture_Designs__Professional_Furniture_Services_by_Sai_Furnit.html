Addressing Common Furniture Issues in Surat's Climate

Surat’s unique climate, with its fluctuating humidity and temperatures, poses challenges to furniture. Common issues include:

Wood warping and cracking: The intense heat and humidity can cause wood to expand and contract, leading to unsightly damage. Fabric fading and mildew: Sunlight and dampness can negatively impact the lifespan and appearance of upholstery. Pest infestations: Certain insects thrive in humid conditions, compromising the structural integrity of your furniture. High repair costs: Neglecting small problems often leads to more extensive (and costly) repairs later.

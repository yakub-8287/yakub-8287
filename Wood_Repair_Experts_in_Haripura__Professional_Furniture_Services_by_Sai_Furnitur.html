2. Common Furniture Problems in Surat: Understanding the Challenges

Surat's hot and humid climate presents significant challenges for wooden furniture. Fluctuations in temperature and humidity can cause wood to expand, contract, and warp, leading to cracks, splits, and structural damage. Common issues include:

Water damage: Spills, leaks, and monsoon season moisture can cause significant damage. Scratches and dents: Daily wear and tear can leave unsightly marks. Loose joints and broken parts: Over time, furniture components can loosen or break. Insect infestation: Wood-boring insects are a common problem in the Surat region. Fading and discoloration: Exposure to sunlight can cause furniture to fade and lose its color.

These issues not only affect the aesthetics of your furniture but can also impact its structural integrity and longevity, incurring costly replacements or repairs down the line. Ignoring these problems can also compromise the safety of your furniture, potentially leading to injuries.

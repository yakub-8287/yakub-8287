The Problem: Worn-Out School Furniture in Surat

Schools in Surat, like those across Gujarat, face unique challenges concerning their furniture. The hot and humid climate contributes significantly to wear and tear. Wooden chairs and desks can warp, crack, or become infested with termites. Metal furniture can rust and become unstable. These issues aren't just aesthetic; they pose safety risks to students and staff. Replacing damaged furniture is a costly undertaking, placing a significant burden on school budgets. Furthermore, disposing of old furniture raises environmental concerns.

Common issues: Cracks, splinters, loose joints, rust, damaged upholstery, termite infestations. Climate impact: High humidity and temperature lead to warping, swelling, and faster deterioration. Safety concerns: Unstable chairs and desks pose tripping hazards and potential injuries.

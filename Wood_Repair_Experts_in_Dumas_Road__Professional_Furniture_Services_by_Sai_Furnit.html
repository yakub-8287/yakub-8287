Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's humid climate and fluctuating temperatures can wreak havoc on wooden furniture. Common problems include:

Cracks and splits: Caused by seasonal changes in humidity. Water damage: From spills or leaks leading to warping or rot. Scratches and dents: From everyday wear and tear. Loose joints and broken legs: Often due to age and stress. Faded finishes: Resulting from sun exposure and general wear.

These issues not only affect the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but can also compromise its structural integrity, potentially leading to safety concerns and costly replacements. Protecting your investment is essential, and prompt repair can significantly extend the lifespan of your beloved pieces.

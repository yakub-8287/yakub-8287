Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's unique climate, characterized by high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, significantly impacts furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and warping: The humid climate can cause wood to absorb moisture, leading to warping, cracking, and even rot. Pest infestation: Termites and other insects thrive in humid conditions, damaging furniture structures. Upholstery damage: Extreme temperatures and humidity can affect the fabric and foam of upholstered furniture. Structural damage: Wear and tear, accidental damage, and poor initial construction can lead to structural instability.

These problems not only diminish the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but can also pose safety risks, and neglecting them can lead to costly replacements.

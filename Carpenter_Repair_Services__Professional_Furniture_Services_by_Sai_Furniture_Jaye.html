Common Furniture Problems in Surat: The Challenges of the Surat Climate

The humid climate and fluctuating temperatures in Surat can wreak havoc on furniture. Common issues we see include:

Wood rot and warping: Humidity causes wood to swell and warp, potentially leading to structural damage. Pest infestations: Warm temperatures create a breeding ground for wood-boring insects. Sun damage: Prolonged exposure to sunlight can fade and crack finishes. Loose joints and broken pieces: Daily use inevitably causes wear and tear.

Ignoring these problems can lead to increased repair costs down the line, or even the complete loss of valuable furniture. Furthermore, damaged furniture can pose safety hazards, creating the potential for injuries.

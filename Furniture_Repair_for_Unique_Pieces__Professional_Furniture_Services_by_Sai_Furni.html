The Challenges Facing Your Furniture in Surat

Surat's climate, characterized by high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, can significantly impact the condition of your furniture. Common issues include wood rot, warping, insect infestations, loose joints, and faded finishes. These problems not only detract from the aesthetic appeal but can also compromise the structural integrity of your pieces, leading to safety concerns and costly repairs down the line. Ignoring minor issues can lead to more extensive and expensive damage. For example, a seemingly small crack in a traditional Gujarati dhokra style chair can quickly escalate into a major structural problem.

Common Furniture Problems in Surat's Humid Climate

Surat's climate, characterized by high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, presents unique challenges for furniture. Common problems include:

Wood rot and warping: The humidity can cause wood to expand and contract, leading to cracking, warping, and rot, particularly in older furniture. Pest infestation: Warm, humid conditions can attract termites and other wood-boring insects. Joint failure: Changes in humidity can loosen joints, causing furniture to become unstable. Upholstery damage: Moisture can damage upholstery fabrics, leading to discoloration, mildew, and structural weakening.

Ignoring these issues can lead to costly repairs down the line and, in some cases, complete furniture loss. Furthermore, attempting DIY repairs without proper expertise can often worsen the problem and even create safety hazards.

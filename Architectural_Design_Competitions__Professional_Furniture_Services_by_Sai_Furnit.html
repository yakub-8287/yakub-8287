Common Furniture Issues in Surat & Their Impact

The humid climate of Surat, coupled with fluctuating temperatures, presents unique challenges for furniture. Common problems include:

Pest infestation: Certain insects thrive in humid conditions, damaging wooden frames and fabrics. Structural damage: Over time, furniture can suffer structural damage from everyday wear and tear.

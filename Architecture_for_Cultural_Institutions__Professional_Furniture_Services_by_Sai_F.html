Common Furniture Issues in Surat's Cultural Institutions

Surat's humid climate and fluctuating temperatures pose significant challenges to the longevity of wooden furniture. Common problems include:

Wood rot and decay: Excessive moisture leads to warping, cracking, and the deterioration of valuable pieces. Pest infestation: Termites and other insects can cause extensive damage if left untreated. Fading and discoloration: Sunlight exposure and humidity can affect the vibrancy of finishes. Structural instability: Age and wear can weaken furniture, posing safety risks.

These issues not only impact the aesthetic appeal of your institution but also incur significant repair costs and compromise the safety of visitors and artifacts.

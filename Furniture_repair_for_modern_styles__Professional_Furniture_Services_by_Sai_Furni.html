Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's climate, with its fluctuating humidity and temperatures, presents unique challenges for furniture. The intense heat can cause wood to warp or crack, while humidity can lead to mildew and swelling. These issues are particularly prevalent with modern furniture, often made from materials sensitive to environmental changes. Furthermore, accidents happen – scratches, stains, broken legs – all impacting the aesthetic appeal and functionality of your furniture. Ignoring these problems can lead to costly replacements and even safety hazards.

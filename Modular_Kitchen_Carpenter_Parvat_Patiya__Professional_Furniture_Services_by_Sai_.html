Common Furniture Problems in Surat's Climate

Surat, with its humid climate and fluctuating temperatures, presents unique challenges for furniture. The intense heat and humidity can lead to:

Wood warping and cracking: Changes in moisture levels can cause wood to expand and contract, leading to structural damage. Pest infestations: Warm, humid conditions create ideal breeding grounds for termites and other wood-boring insects. Fading and discoloration: Prolonged sun exposure can cause furniture finishes to fade and lose their vibrancy. Hardware failure: Frequent temperature changes can cause screws and hinges to loosen or break.

These issues not only impact the aesthetics of your furniture but also pose safety risks and can lead to costly replacements.

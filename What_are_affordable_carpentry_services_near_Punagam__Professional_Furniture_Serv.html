The Problem: Furniture Woes in Surat's Humid Climate

Surat, with its unique climate, presents several challenges for your furniture. The high humidity and fluctuating temperatures can lead to several common problems:

Warped wood: Changes in moisture content can cause wood to warp, crack, and lose its structural integrity. This is particularly common with older furniture pieces. Pest infestation: The humid climate can attract termites and other wood-boring insects, leading to costly repairs or even replacement. Fading and damage: Direct sunlight and exposure to the elements can cause furniture finishes to fade and become damaged. Broken joints and loose fittings: Years of use can weaken joints, leading to instability and safety hazards.

These issues not only affect the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but can also pose safety risks. Repairing these problems yourself can be difficult and often leads to more damage, increasing the overall cost.

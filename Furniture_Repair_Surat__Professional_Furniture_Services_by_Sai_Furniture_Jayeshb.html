Common Furniture Problems in Surat: The Challenges of Gujarat's Climate

Surat's hot and humid climate poses significant challenges to furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and damage: High humidity can lead to warping, cracking, and fungal growth in wooden furniture, particularly in older pieces or those made from less durable woods. Fabric wear and tear: Intense sunlight and humidity can fade fabrics and cause them to become brittle. Pest infestations: Warm weather can attract pests such as termites and wood borers that can cause significant damage if left untreated. Structural instability: Years of use and exposure to the elements can weaken furniture's structural integrity, making it unsafe to use.

Ignoring these problems can lead to costly replacements and potential safety hazards.

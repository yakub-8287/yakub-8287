2. Common Furniture Problems in Surat: Understanding the Challenges

Surat's climate, with its fluctuating humidity and temperatures, poses significant challenges to furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and warping: The high humidity can lead to wood swelling, cracking, and even rot, particularly in older furniture pieces. Upholstery damage: Spills, tears, and wear and tear are common problems affecting the upholstery of sofas, chairs, and other upholstered furniture.

2. Problem Statement: Furniture Challenges in Surat's Climate

Surat, with its humid climate and fluctuating temperatures, presents unique challenges for furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and warping: High humidity can cause wood to expand and crack, damaging both aesthetic appeal and structural integrity. Pest infestation: Warm weather creates ideal conditions for termites and other wood-boring insects. Fabric deterioration: Heat and humidity can fade and damage upholstery fabrics. Metal corrosion: Exposure to moisture can lead to rust and corrosion in metal furniture. High replacement costs: Replacing damaged furniture can significantly increase development costs.

These issues not only impact the aesthetic quality of your building but can also pose safety hazards. Proactive furniture care is crucial for maintaining both beauty and safety within your mixed-use development.

Common Furniture Problems in Surat's Climate

Surat's humid climate and fluctuating temperatures can wreak havoc on furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and decay: Humidity causes wood to absorb moisture, leading to warping, cracking, and eventually rot. Pest infestations: Warm, humid conditions create ideal breeding grounds for termites and other wood-boring insects. Fabric damage: Moisture can cause mold and mildew to grow on upholstery, ruining its appearance and integrity. Loose joints and broken components: Wear and tear, along with shifting temperatures, can loosen joints and cause furniture to become unstable.

Ignoring these problems can lead to costly repairs down the line and even pose safety risks.

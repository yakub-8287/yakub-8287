Common Furniture Problems in Surat's Climate

Surat's humid climate and fluctuating temperatures can wreak havoc on your furniture. Common problems include:

Wood rot and warping: Excessive moisture weakens the wood's structure, leading to decay and distortion. Pest infestations: Warm, humid conditions can attract termites and other wood-boring insects. Cracked or damaged finishes: Temperature changes can cause paint or varnish to crack and peel. Loose joints and broken components: Years of use and wear can lead to structural instability.

These issues not only compromise the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but can also pose safety risks and lead to costly replacements.

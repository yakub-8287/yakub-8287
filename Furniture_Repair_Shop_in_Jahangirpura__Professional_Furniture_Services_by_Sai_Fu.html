Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's warm and humid climate presents unique challenges for furniture. The fluctuating temperatures and high humidity can lead to several issues:

Wood warping and cracking: Changes in humidity cause wood to expand and contract, leading to warping, cracking, and splitting. Pest infestation: Warm, humid conditions can attract insects like termites and wood borers, causing significant damage. Upholstery damage: Moisture can affect the fabric and foam of upholstery, leading to mold, mildew, and structural damage. Loose joints and broken parts: Years of use can cause joints to loosen and parts to break, especially in older furniture.

These issues not only detract from the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but can also pose safety hazards and lead to costly replacements.

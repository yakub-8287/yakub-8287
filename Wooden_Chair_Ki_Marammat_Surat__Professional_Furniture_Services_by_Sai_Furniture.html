The Problem with Damaged Wooden Chairs in Surat

Surat's unique climate, with its high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, can significantly impact the condition of wooden furniture. Exposure to the elements or even simple wear and tear can lead to several issues:

Cracked or Split Wood: Humidity changes cause wood to expand and contract, leading to cracks and splits.

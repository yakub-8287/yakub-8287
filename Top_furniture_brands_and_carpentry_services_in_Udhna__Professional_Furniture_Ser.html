Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's climate, with its humidity and fluctuating temperatures, can significantly impact your furniture. Common issues include:

Wood warping and cracking: Changes in humidity can cause wood to expand and contract, leading to cracks and warping. Pest infestation: Warm, humid conditions can attract termites and other pests that damage wooden furniture. Upholstery damage: Wear and tear, spills, and sun exposure can affect the fabric and integrity of upholstered pieces. Loose joints and broken components: Regular use can weaken joints and lead to breakage.

These problems not only affect the aesthetics of your furniture but can also compromise its structural integrity and pose safety risks. Ignoring these issues can lead to costly repairs down the line.

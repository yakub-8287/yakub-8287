2. Problem Statement: The Challenges of Outdoor Furniture in Surat

Surat's climate, characterized by intense heat, humidity, and occasional monsoon rains, takes a toll on outdoor furniture. Common problems include:

Wood rot and decay: Moisture damage weakens the structural integrity of wooden furniture. Weakened wicker and rattan: These materials can become brittle and break easily under the elements. Faded fabrics and cushions: Prolonged sun exposure causes discoloration and deterioration. Rust and corrosion: Metal furniture is susceptible to rusting in humid conditions.

Ignoring these issues can lead to costly replacements and even create safety hazards. Preventative furniture repair and restoration are crucial investments for preserving your outdoor furniture in the long run.

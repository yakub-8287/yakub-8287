The Challenges of Furniture Maintenance in Surat

Surat, with its vibrant culture and unique climate, presents specific challenges for furniture upkeep. The humidity and fluctuating temperatures common in Gujarat can cause wood to warp, crack, and degrade over time. This is particularly true for traditional Gujarati furniture styles often crafted from local woods. Ignoring these issues can lead to costly repairs down the line and even compromise safety. Cracked chairs or unstable tables pose significant risks, especially in homes with children.

Warping and cracking of wood due to humidity Loose joints and structural instability Pest infestation (termite damage) Scratches and other cosmetic damage Outdated designs requiring refurbishment

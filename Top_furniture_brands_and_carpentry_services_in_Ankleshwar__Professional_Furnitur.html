Common Furniture Problems in Surat and Ankleshwar

Surat, like many areas in Gujarat, experiences significant variations in temperature and humidity. This can lead to several common furniture problems:

Wood warping and cracking: Fluctuations in humidity can cause wood to expand and contract, leading to cracks and warping, especially in older pieces. Pest infestation: Warm, humid conditions create ideal breeding grounds for wood-boring insects, damaging furniture and compromising its structural integrity. Upholstery damage: Excessive heat and humidity can cause upholstery fabrics to fade, mildew, and deteriorate. Loose joints and broken parts: Regular wear and tear, combined with environmental factors, can weaken furniture joints, resulting in instability and safety hazards. These issues can be costly to fix, potentially requiring replacement if neglected.

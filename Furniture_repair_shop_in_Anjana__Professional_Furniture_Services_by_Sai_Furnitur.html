2. Common Furniture Problems in Surat: Understanding the Challenges

Surat's climate, with its fluctuating humidity and temperatures, poses significant challenges for furniture. Common problems include:

Wood rot and warping: Excessive humidity can cause wood to swell, crack, and warp, damaging its structural integrity. Insect infestation: Certain insects thrive in humid conditions and can infest wooden furniture, causing significant damage. Fabric wear and tear: The heat and humidity can affect fabric upholstery, leading to fading, staining, and deterioration. Loose joints and broken components: Years of use can take their toll, leading to structural weakness and damage.

These issues not only diminish the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but can also lead to costly replacements and even safety hazards.

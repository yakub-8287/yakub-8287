Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's humid climate and fluctuating temperatures can take a toll on your furniture. Common issues include:

Pest infestations: Warm, humid conditions can attract termites and other wood-boring insects. Upholstery damage: Moisture can damage fabric, causing discoloration, mildew, and even structural weakness. Loose joints and broken components: Years of use and wear can lead to structural damage.

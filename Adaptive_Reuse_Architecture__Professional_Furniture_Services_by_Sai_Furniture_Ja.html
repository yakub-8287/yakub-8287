Surat's climate, characterized by high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, poses unique challenges to furniture. Replacing damaged pieces can be expensive, not to mention the emotional cost of losing a family heirloom. Furthermore, poorly repaired furniture can pose safety hazards, leading to injuries or further damage. Many find themselves struggling to maintain the quality and integrity of their treasured pieces within the city's environmental conditions.

Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's climate, characterized by high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, presents unique challenges to furniture. Common problems include:

Wood rot and warping: Excessive moisture can lead to wood degradation and structural damage. Cracked and chipped surfaces: Extreme temperature changes cause expansion and contraction, resulting in cracks and chips in wood and veneer. Pest infestations: Warm and humid conditions can attract insects, damaging the structural integrity of your furniture. Upholstery wear and tear: Regular use, coupled with humidity, leads to fading, tearing, and staining of fabric.

These issues not only diminish the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but can also pose safety risks and lead to costly replacements.

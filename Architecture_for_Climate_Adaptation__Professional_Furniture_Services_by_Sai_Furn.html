The Problem: Surat's Climate and its Impact on Furniture

Surat’s climate, characterized by intense heat, humidity, and monsoons, wreaks havoc on furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and warping: Excessive moisture leads to deterioration of wood, causing structural damage and unsightly warping, particularly noticeable in traditional Gujarati furniture styles. Fading and discoloration: Prolonged sun exposure fades fabrics and finishes, diminishing the aesthetic appeal of your pieces. Pest infestations: Warm and humid conditions create ideal breeding grounds for insects, leading to damage and costly repairs. Structural instability: Exposure to extreme temperatures can weaken the structural integrity of furniture, leading to safety hazards.

These problems not only diminish the beauty of your furniture but also lead to significant financial burdens through costly replacements or extensive repairs.

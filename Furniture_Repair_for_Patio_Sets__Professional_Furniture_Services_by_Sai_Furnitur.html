Common Patio Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's hot and humid climate presents unique challenges for outdoor furniture. Common problems include:

Sun-damaged wood: Fading, cracking, and splintering due to prolonged sun exposure. Weakened wicker or rattan: Deterioration from moisture and UV rays. Rusty metal frames: Corrosion from humidity and rain. Torn or faded cushions: Wear and tear from regular use and exposure to the elements. Loose joints and wobbly legs: General wear and tear from use.

Ignoring these issues can lead to costly replacements and even safety hazards. For example, a wobbly chair can lead to injury, while rotting wood might pose a risk to children playing nearby.

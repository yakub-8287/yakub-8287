Common Furniture Issues in Surat and Their Solutions

Surat, with its unique climate, presents challenges for preserving furniture. High humidity and fluctuating temperatures can lead to:

Wood rot and warping: The humid climate can cause wood to absorb moisture, leading to rot and warping, common in older, handcrafted pieces. Cracks and splits: Temperature fluctuations can cause stress on the wood, resulting in cracks and splits. Insect infestation: Certain pests thrive in humid conditions, damaging furniture structures. Finish deterioration: The lacquer or varnish on antique pieces can chip or fade over time.

Ignoring these problems can lead to irreversible damage and significant financial loss. Investing in professional furniture repair for collectibles is a wise precaution.

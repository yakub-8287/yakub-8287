Common Furniture Problems in Surat & Their Impact

Surat's climate, with its fluctuating humidity and temperatures, can significantly impact furniture. Common issues we encounter include:

Wood rot and warping: High humidity can lead to wood expansion and subsequent cracking or warping, especially in side tables made of solid wood. Veneer lifting: Changes in temperature and humidity can cause veneer to separate from the underlying substrate. Scratch and scuff marks: Everyday wear and tear leaves its mark, particularly on side tables in high-traffic areas. Loose joints and wobbly legs: Over time, the joints in your side table may loosen, leading to instability.

These problems not only impact the aesthetics of your furniture but can also pose safety risks and ultimately lead to costly replacements. Repairing your furniture is a far more economical and sustainable solution.

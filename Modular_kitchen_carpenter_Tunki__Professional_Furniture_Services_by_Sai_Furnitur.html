The Challenges of Furniture in Surat's Climate

Surat, with its unique climate, presents specific challenges to furniture. The high humidity and fluctuating temperatures can lead to several issues:

Warped wood: Changes in moisture content can cause wood to expand and contract, leading to warping and cracking. Pest infestation: The warm, humid climate creates ideal conditions for pests like termites and borers. Fading finishes: Sunlight and humidity can cause furniture finishes to fade and become dull. Structural damage: Extreme weather conditions can compromise the structural integrity of furniture.

These problems can result in costly repairs or even necessitate complete replacements, impacting both your budget and your home's aesthetic appeal.

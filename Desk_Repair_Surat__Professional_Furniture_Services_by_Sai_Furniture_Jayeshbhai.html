Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's humid climate and fluctuating temperatures can wreak havoc on wooden furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and warping: Excessive moisture can cause wood to swell, crack, and even rot, particularly in older desks. Scratching and damage: Daily wear and tear from use inevitably leads to surface scratches and chips. Loose joints and wobbly legs: Over time, joints can loosen, causing desks to become unstable and unsafe. Damaged veneers: Veneered surfaces are susceptible to peeling and damage, compromising the desk's appearance.

Ignoring these issues not only impacts the aesthetics of your office but can also compromise its safety and functionality, potentially leading to higher replacement costs in the long run.

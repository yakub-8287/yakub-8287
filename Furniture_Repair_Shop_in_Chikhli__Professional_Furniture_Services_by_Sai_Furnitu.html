Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's climate, with its high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, can take a toll on your furniture. Common problems include:

Wood rot and decay: Excessive moisture can cause wood to rot, weakening its structure and leading to cracking. Mildew and mold: Humid conditions create ideal breeding grounds for these damaging organisms, affecting both the wood and upholstery. Cracks and splits: Temperature changes can cause wood to expand and contract, leading to cracks and splits in furniture frames. Loose joints and broken parts: Years of use can weaken furniture joints, causing them to loosen or break. Upholstery damage: Tears, stains, and general wear and tear are common problems affecting upholstery.

These problems can not only impact the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but also pose safety risks and significant repair costs. Ignoring minor issues can lead to more extensive and costly repairs down the line.

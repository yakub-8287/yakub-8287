Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's humid climate and fluctuating temperatures can significantly impact the lifespan and condition of your furniture. Common issues include:

Fabric deterioration: Humidity can cause mildew and fading of upholstery. Loose joints and broken components: General wear and tear can compromise structural integrity.

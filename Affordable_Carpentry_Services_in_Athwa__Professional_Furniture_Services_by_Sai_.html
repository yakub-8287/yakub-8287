Common Furniture Problems in Surat & Athwa

Surat's climate, with its high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, presents several challenges for furniture. Common issues include:

Wood warping and cracking: The humidity can cause wood to swell and shrink, leading to cracks and warping. Pest infestation: Termites and other insects are attracted to wooden furniture, causing significant damage. Loose joints and broken parts: Regular use and wear and tear can weaken joints and break furniture components. Upholstery wear and tear: Sun exposure and regular use can fade and damage upholstery.

These issues not only impact the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but also pose safety risks. Ignoring these problems can lead to expensive repairs later on. A timely intervention by professionals is often the most economical approach.

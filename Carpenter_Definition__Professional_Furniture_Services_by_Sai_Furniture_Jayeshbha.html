Common Furniture Problems Faced by Surat Residents

Surat's unique climate, with its high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, poses several challenges for furniture. Common problems include:

Wood rot and warping: The humid Surat summers can cause wooden furniture to swell, warp, and even rot if not properly maintained. Pest infestations: Termites and other wood-boring insects are a significant concern, requiring prompt and professional intervention. Cracked and broken joints: Daily use and accidental damage can lead to structural issues, affecting both the functionality and aesthetics of your furniture. Outdated styles: While Surat boasts a vibrant mix of traditional and modern furniture styles, older pieces might require updating or restoration to match contemporary tastes.

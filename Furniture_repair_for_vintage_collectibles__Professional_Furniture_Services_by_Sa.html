The Challenges of Vintage Furniture in Surat's Climate

Surat's hot and humid climate presents unique challenges for vintage furniture. Wood can warp, crack, or become infested with pests. Changes in temperature can cause finishes to peel or crack. Ignoring these issues can lead to irreversible damage, significantly impacting the value and safety of your collectibles. Moreover, improper repairs can further compromise the structural integrity of your antique furniture, potentially leading to costly replacements.

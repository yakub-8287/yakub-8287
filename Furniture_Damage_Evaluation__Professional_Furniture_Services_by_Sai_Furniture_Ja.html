Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's hot and humid climate presents unique challenges for furniture. Common issues we encounter include:

Wood rot and warping: High humidity can lead to wood expanding and warping, causing cracks and structural damage. Mildew and mold growth: Damp conditions create the perfect breeding ground for mildew and mold, affecting both the aesthetics and structural integrity. Insect infestation: Certain insects thrive in humid conditions, leading to infestations that damage wood and upholstery. Upholstery damage: Spills, tears, and general wear and tear are common, especially in high-traffic areas. Scratches and dents: Everyday accidents can lead to minor to significant cosmetic damage.

These issues not only affect the appearance of your furniture but can also significantly impact its value and even pose safety risks. Ignoring these problems can lead to costly repairs down the line.

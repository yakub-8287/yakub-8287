Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's climate, characterized by high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, poses significant challenges to furniture. Wood can warp, crack, or become infested with insects. Common problems include:

Wood rot and decay: Dampness leads to deterioration of wooden components. Cracked or broken joints: Years of use can weaken structural integrity. Damaged veneers and inlays: These delicate details are prone to chipping and damage. Faded finishes and worn upholstery: Sunlight and everyday use can dull the vibrancy of your antique furniture.

Ignoring these problems can lead to increased repair costs and even complete loss of your precious items. Moreover, damaged furniture can pose safety hazards, such as loose joints and splintered wood.

The Problem: Pet Damage and Surat's Climate

Surat's unique climate, with its fluctuating humidity and temperatures, can exacerbate existing furniture damage caused by pets. Scratches, stains, and tears can quickly worsen, leading to costly replacements. Common problems we see include:

Claw marks: Cats and dogs leave noticeable scratches on sofas, chairs, and wooden furniture. Fabric tears: Excited pets can easily rip fabric upholstery. Ignoring these issues can lead to further damage, making repairs more extensive and expensive. It's crucial to address pet-related damage quickly for both aesthetic and structural integrity.

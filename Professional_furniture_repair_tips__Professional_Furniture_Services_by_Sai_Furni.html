Common Furniture Problems in Surat: A Humid Reality

Surat's humid climate and fluctuating temperatures can significantly impact your furniture's condition. Common problems include:

Wood rot and warping: High humidity can lead to wood absorbing excessive moisture, causing warping and rot, particularly in older pieces. Pest infestation: Warm temperatures can attract wood-boring insects, leading to structural damage. Fabric damage: Humidity and dust can lead to mildew and staining on upholstered furniture. Loose joints and broken components: Regular wear and tear, combined with environmental factors, can loosen joints and cause breakage.

Ignoring these issues can lead to costly replacements and potential safety hazards.

2. Common Furniture Problems in Surat's Climate

Surat's climate, characterized by high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, presents unique challenges for furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and decay: Excessive moisture weakens wood structures, leading to warping, cracking, and infestations. Pest infestations: Humidity can attract termites and other wood-boring insects, damaging your valuable furniture. Fading and discoloration: The intense sun can cause furniture fabrics and finishes to fade prematurely. Structural damage: Seasonal temperature changes can cause wood to expand and contract, leading to cracks and instability. This is especially apparent in older furniture.

These problems not only impact the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but also carry significant financial implications, potentially requiring costly replacements. Moreover, dealing with pest infestations can present health and safety risks.

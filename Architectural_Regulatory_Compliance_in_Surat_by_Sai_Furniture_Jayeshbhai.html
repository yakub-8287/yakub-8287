2. Problem Statement: Common Furniture Issues in Surat

Surat's climate, characterized by high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, significantly impacts furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and damage: The humid climate can cause wood to warp, rot, and become structurally unsound, posing safety risks and potentially violating building codes. Pest infestations: Warm, humid conditions create ideal breeding grounds for termites and other wood-boring insects, leading to extensive damage and costly repairs. Deterioration of finishes: High humidity can cause paint and varnish to peel, requiring costly refinishing to maintain aesthetic appeal and structural integrity. Structural instability: Damaged or compromised furniture can create safety hazards and potentially lead to non-compliance with building regulations.

These issues can not only impact the aesthetic value of your property but also lead to significant financial burdens and safety concerns.

2. Common Furniture Problems in Surat: The Challenges of our Climate

Surat's humid climate and fluctuating temperatures pose significant challenges to furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and warping: Excess moisture can cause wood to swell, crack, and eventually rot, damaging the structural integrity of your furniture. Fading and discoloration: Prolonged exposure to sunlight can lead to fading and discoloration, especially in fabrics and finishes. Pest infestation: Termites and other wood-boring insects thrive in humid conditions, causing significant damage to wooden furniture. Loose joints and broken components: Everyday wear and tear can loosen joints and break components, compromising the functionality of your furniture.

These problems can be costly to repair if left unattended, often leading to premature furniture replacement. Furthermore, damaged furniture can pose safety risks, especially if components are unstable or sharp edges are exposed.

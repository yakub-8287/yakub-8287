Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's hot and humid climate presents unique challenges for furniture. The intense heat can cause wood to warp and crack, while humidity leads to mold growth and damage to upholstery. Termites are also a common concern, leading to structural damage. These problems not only affect the aesthetics of your furniture but can also pose safety risks. Ignoring these issues can lead to significant costs in the long run, requiring complete furniture replacement instead of simple repair. Common issues include broken legs, damaged upholstery, loose joints, and termite infestations.

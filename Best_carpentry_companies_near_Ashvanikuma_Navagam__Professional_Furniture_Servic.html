Common Furniture Problems in Surat & Their Solutions

Surat's hot and humid climate poses unique challenges for furniture. The fluctuating temperatures and high humidity can lead to:

Wood warping and cracking: Extreme changes in temperature and moisture content cause wood to expand and contract, leading to cracks and warping. Pest infestations: Warm, humid environments can attract termites and other wood-boring insects, damaging your furniture. Fading and discoloration: Sunlight can cause furniture to fade and lose its original vibrancy, particularly in areas with direct sunlight exposure. Loose joints and broken components: Years of wear and tear can weaken joints and lead to breakage.

These issues can not only diminish the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but also lead to significant repair costs if not addressed promptly and professionally. Ignoring these problems can also compromise safety, especially with damaged chairs or tables.

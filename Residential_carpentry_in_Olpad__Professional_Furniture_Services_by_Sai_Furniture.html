Common Furniture Problems in Surat & Olpad

Surat's climate, characterized by high humidity and temperature fluctuations, takes a toll on furniture. Common problems include:

Wood rot and decay: Moisture damage weakens the structural integrity of your furniture. Cracks and splits: Extreme temperature changes cause wood to expand and contract, leading to cracks. Pest infestations: Warm, humid conditions attract termites and other wood-boring insects. Worn-out finishes: Regular wear and tear dulls the appearance of your furniture. Outdated styles: Your cherished pieces might no longer reflect your current taste.

These issues not only affect the aesthetic appeal of your home but can also pose safety hazards and lead to costly replacements.

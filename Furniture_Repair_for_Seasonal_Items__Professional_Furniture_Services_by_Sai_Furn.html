2. Problem Statement: The Challenges Facing Seasonal Furniture in Surat

Surat's climate poses unique challenges to seasonal furniture. High humidity can lead to warping, cracking, and the growth of mold and mildew, particularly on wooden pieces. Extreme temperatures can cause paint to peel, fabrics to fade, and wood to expand or contract, potentially damaging joints and structures. Ignoring these issues can lead to costly repairs down the line, or even the complete loss of your valued furniture. Safety is also a concern; damaged furniture can become unstable, posing a risk to you and your family.

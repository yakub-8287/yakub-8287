The Problem: Furniture Issues in Surat's Unique Climate

Surat's climate, with its fluctuating humidity and temperatures, presents unique challenges for furniture. Common issues include:

Pest infestations: Warm, humid conditions create ideal breeding grounds for wood-boring insects, damaging the structural integrity of your furniture. Fabric fading and deterioration: Exposure to sunlight and humidity can quickly fade fabrics and cause deterioration in upholstery. Costly replacements: Neglecting these issues often leads to more expensive repairs or even the need to replace furniture altogether.

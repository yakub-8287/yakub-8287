Common Furniture Problems in Surat

The hot and humid Surat climate presents unique challenges for your furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and warping: Excess moisture can cause wood to expand, crack, and rot, especially in older furniture pieces. Pest infestation: Warm, humid conditions can attract insects like termites and borers, damaging wooden furniture. Fading and discoloration: Sunlight and humidity can cause fabrics and finishes to fade and lose their vibrancy. Structural damage: Over time, furniture can suffer from wear and tear, resulting in loose joints, broken legs, or damaged upholstery.

Ignoring these problems can lead to costly repairs and even total replacement.

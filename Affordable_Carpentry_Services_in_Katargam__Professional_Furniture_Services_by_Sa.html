Common Furniture Problems in Surat & Their Solutions

Surat, with its humid climate and varying temperatures, can take a toll on your furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and warping: The humidity can cause wood to expand and contract, leading to warping, cracking, and ultimately, rot. Loose joints and broken components: Constant use, coupled with the effects of the climate, often leads to weakened joints and broken parts. Pest infestation: Termites and other wood-boring insects are a significant problem in many parts of Surat. Scratches and damage: Everyday wear and tear can leave your furniture looking less than its best.

Common Furniture Problems in Surat & Bharuch

The humid climate of Surat and Bharuch, coupled with fluctuating temperatures, takes its toll on furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and damage: Excess moisture leads to warping, cracking, and fungal growth. Upholstery wear and tear: Sun exposure and frequent use damage fabric and cushions. Loose joints and broken parts: General wear and tear weakens the structure over time. Pest infestation: Termites and other insects can cause significant damage. Scratches and dents: Daily use inevitably leads to cosmetic damage.

Ignoring these problems can lead to significant expenses down the line. Ignoring termite damage, for example, can lead to complete furniture replacement, whereas early intervention could be a simple fix. Furthermore, unsafe, poorly repaired furniture poses a risk to household members.

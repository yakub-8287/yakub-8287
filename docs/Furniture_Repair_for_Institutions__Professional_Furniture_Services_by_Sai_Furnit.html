Common Furniture Issues Facing Surat Institutions

The hot and humid climate of Surat, Gujarat, presents unique challenges to furniture. Common problems include:

Wood rot and termite damage: The damp conditions can lead to wood decay and infestation. Fabric wear and tear: High usage in educational and healthcare settings leads to rapid deterioration of upholstery. Structural damage: Heavy use can damage chair frames, table legs, and other structural elements. Sun damage: Prolonged exposure to sunlight can fade finishes and damage wood. Vandalism: Schools and public spaces are unfortunately susceptible to intentional damage.

These issues not only affect aesthetics but also pose safety risks and can lead to significant replacement costs. Repairing existing furniture is often more cost-effective than complete replacements, thereby saving institutions valuable resources.

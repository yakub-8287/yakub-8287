2. The Problem: Furniture Issues in Surat's Climate

Surat's hot and humid climate poses significant challenges to furniture. The high humidity can lead to warping, cracking, and pest infestations, particularly in wooden furniture. Temperature fluctuations can cause expansion and contraction, damaging joints and finishes. These issues not only impact the aesthetics but also the structural integrity, leading to costly replacements or unsafe conditions. Furthermore, discarding damaged furniture contributes to landfill waste, impacting the environment. Repairing and restoring furniture is significantly more economical than replacing it, and it's better for the planet too.

Common Furniture Problems in Surat

The humid climate of Surat, coupled with fluctuating temperatures, presents unique challenges for furniture. Common issues we encounter include:

Wood rot and warping: Excess moisture can cause wood to expand and crack, leading to structural damage. Polishing and finish damage: Scratches, chips, and fading are common problems caused by daily use and environmental factors. Upholstery wear and tear: Fabrics can fade, tear, and become stained over time, impacting both appearance and comfort. Loose joints and broken pieces: Regular wear and tear can loosen joints or even cause complete breakage. Pest infestation: Certain wood-boring insects thrive in humid environments and can cause extensive damage.

These issues not only affect the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but can also pose safety risks and lead to costly replacements.

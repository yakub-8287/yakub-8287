2. The Problem: Common Furniture Issues in Surat

Surat's humid climate and fluctuating temperatures can significantly impact your furniture. Common problems include:

Wood rot and damage: Humidity can lead to warping, cracking, and the growth of mold and mildew in wooden furniture. Fading and discoloration: Exposure to sunlight can cause fabrics and finishes to fade over time, diminishing the beauty of your pieces. Structural damage: Years of use can lead to loose joints, broken legs, or other structural issues. Pest infestation: Certain insects and pests can cause significant damage to untreated wood.

These problems not only affect the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but also impact its value and safety. Ignoring these issues can lead to more costly repairs in the long run.

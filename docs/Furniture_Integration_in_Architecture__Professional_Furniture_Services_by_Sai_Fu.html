Common Furniture Challenges in Surat's Climate

Surat's climate, with its periods of high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, presents unique challenges for furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and warping: The humid summers can cause wood to swell and warp, compromising structural integrity. Pest infestation: Certain insects thrive in humid conditions, leading to damage and decay of furniture. Fabric deterioration: High humidity can lead to mildew and mold growth, affecting the lifespan of upholstered pieces. Metal corrosion: Fluctuating humidity levels can cause metal furniture to rust or corrode.

The Challenges of Furniture in Surat's Climate

Surat, with its hot and humid climate, presents unique challenges to furniture. High humidity can lead to warping, swelling, and cracking in wooden furniture, while fluctuating temperatures can cause expansion and contraction, leading to damage and instability. These problems are particularly noticeable in kitchens, where moisture and temperature changes are most pronounced. Ignoring these issues can result in costly repairs down the line, potentially compromising the safety and aesthetics of your home. Many homes in the Dungri area, for example, face these issues with their older kitchen units.

Common Furniture Problems in Surat's Climate

Surat's hot and humid climate presents unique challenges for wooden furniture. Exposure to fluctuating temperatures and high humidity can lead to several issues:

Cracks and splits: Changes in moisture content cause wood to expand and contract, resulting in unsightly cracks. Warping and bowing: Uneven exposure to moisture can cause furniture to warp or bow, compromising its structural integrity. Pest infestation: Humidity creates ideal conditions for termites and other wood-boring insects. Fading and discoloration: Sunlight and exposure to the elements can fade the finish and discolor your precious wooden pieces. Scratches and damages: Everyday wear and tear also lead to aesthetic issues in your furniture.

These problems not only diminish the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but can also significantly impact its value and longevity. Ignoring these issues can lead to costly repairs down the line and even unsafe conditions.

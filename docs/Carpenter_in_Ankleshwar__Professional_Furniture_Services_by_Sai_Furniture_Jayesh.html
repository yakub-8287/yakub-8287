Common Furniture Problems in Surat's Climate

The hot and humid climate of Surat, Gujarat, can take a toll on your furniture. Common problems include:

Wood warping and cracking: Fluctuations in temperature and humidity cause wood to expand and contract, leading to warping, cracking, and damage. Pest infestation: Warm, humid conditions create ideal breeding grounds for termites and other wood-boring insects. Finish damage: Sunlight and humidity can fade finishes and cause peeling or discoloration. Loose joints and broken components: Years of use, combined with the climate's effects, can weaken joints and cause furniture to become unstable.

These issues not only affect the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but can also pose safety hazards and lead to costly replacements.

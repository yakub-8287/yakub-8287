Common Furniture Problems in Surat & Their Solutions

Surat's climate, with its high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, presents unique challenges for furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and warping: The humid climate can lead to significant damage if not addressed promptly. Insect infestation: Termites and other pests are prevalent, threatening the structural integrity of your furniture. Cracks and splits: Changes in temperature can cause wood to expand and contract, leading to unsightly cracks. Damaged upholstery: Exposure to moisture and wear and tear can deteriorate fabric and cushions.

Ignoring these problems can lead to costly repairs or even complete furniture replacement down the line. Addressing these issues early is key to maintaining the value and longevity of your furniture.

Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's climate, with its fluctuating humidity and temperature, presents unique challenges for furniture. Common problems include:

Wood rot and warping: Excessive moisture can lead to wood rot, warping, and cracking in furniture. Pest infestation: Warm and humid conditions can attract pests that damage wood and upholstery. Upholstery wear and tear: Regular use and exposure to sunlight can cause upholstery to fade, tear, and become uncomfortable. Loose joints and broken parts: Years of use can weaken joints and lead to breakage.

These issues not only impact the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but also pose safety concerns and can lead to costly replacements.

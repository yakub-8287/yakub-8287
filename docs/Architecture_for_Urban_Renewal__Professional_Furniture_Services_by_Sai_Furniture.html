Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's humid climate and fluctuating temperatures pose significant challenges for furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and warping: High humidity can cause wood to swell, warp, and rot, especially in older pieces. Pest infestation: Termites and other insects thrive in humid conditions, causing structural damage. Upholstery damage: Moisture can lead to mold and mildew growth in upholstery, impacting both its appearance and lifespan. Fabric fading: Intense sunlight can fade the color of fabrics, particularly in areas with large windows facing south.

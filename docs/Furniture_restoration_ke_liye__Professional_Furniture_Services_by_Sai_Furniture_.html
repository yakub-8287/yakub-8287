Common Furniture Problems in Surat & Their Solutions

Surat's humid climate and fluctuating temperatures can wreak havoc on furniture. Common issues include:

Cracks and splits: Changes in temperature can cause wood to expand and contract, resulting in cracks. Fading and discoloration: Sun exposure can fade finishes and discolor upholstery. Pest infestation: Warm, humid conditions can attract insects, damaging furniture.

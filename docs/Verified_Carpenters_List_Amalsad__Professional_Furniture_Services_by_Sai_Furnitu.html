Common Furniture Problems in Surat

The humid climate of Surat and fluctuating temperatures can significantly impact your furniture. Common problems include:

Wood rot and warping: High humidity leads to moisture absorption, causing wood to rot, warp, and become structurally unsound. Pest infestation: Termites and other wood-boring insects thrive in humid conditions, damaging furniture frames and components. Cracks and splits: Changes in temperature and humidity can cause wood to crack or split, affecting both aesthetics and structural integrity. Loose joints and broken components: Everyday wear and tear, coupled with environmental factors, can lead to loose joints and broken pieces.

These issues can not only impact the beauty of your furniture but also incur significant repair costs if left unaddressed. Ignoring these problems can also lead to safety hazards, such as unstable chairs or damaged shelving units.

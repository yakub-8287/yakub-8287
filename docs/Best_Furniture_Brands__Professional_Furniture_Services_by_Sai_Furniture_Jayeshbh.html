Common Furniture Problems in Surat: The Challenges of Our Climate

Surat's humid climate and fluctuating temperatures pose significant threats to your furniture. Monsoons can cause warping and mildew, while intense summer heat can lead to cracking and fading. These issues not only affect the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but also compromise its structural integrity and safety. Ignoring these problems can lead to costly replacements down the line. Common issues we encounter include:

Water damage from monsoon rains Wood rot and insect infestation Sun-bleached finishes and fading upholstery Sagging frames and broken joints

These problems can pose safety risks, especially with older pieces. Professional furniture repair is crucial for maintaining the safety and value of your investments.

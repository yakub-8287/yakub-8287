The Challenges of Furniture in Surat's Climate

Surat's hot and humid climate presents unique challenges for furniture. The fluctuating temperatures and high humidity can lead to warping, cracking, and pest infestations. These issues not only affect the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but can also compromise its structural integrity, leading to costly repairs down the line. Ignoring these problems can even lead to safety hazards. For example, a weakened chair leg could unexpectedly collapse, causing injury.

2. The Problem with DIY Furniture Assembly in Surat

Assembling bespoke furniture yourself can lead to a number of issues, particularly in Surat's climate. The intense heat and humidity can impact the integrity of wood and other materials, causing warping or cracking. Incorrect assembly can lead to instability, wobbly legs, and even safety hazards.

Cost implications: Damaged furniture might require costly repairs or replacements. Incorrect assembly could void warranties.

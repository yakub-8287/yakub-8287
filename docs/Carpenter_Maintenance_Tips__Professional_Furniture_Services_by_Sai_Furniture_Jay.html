Common Furniture Problems in Surat: The Challenges

Surat's climate presents unique challenges for furniture. High humidity can lead to wood warping, mold growth, and loose joints. Fluctuating temperatures contribute to cracking and splitting. Ignoring these issues can result in costly repairs or even total replacement. Ignoring minor damage can lead to significant problems down the line, impacting both the aesthetic appeal and structural integrity of your pieces. Safety concerns can also arise from damaged furniture, posing risks to children and adults. For example, a wobbly chair or a broken table leg could cause injury.

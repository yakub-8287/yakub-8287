Common Furniture Issues in Surat & Their Solutions

Surat's hot and humid climate presents unique challenges to furniture. High humidity can lead to warping, cracking, and the growth of mold and mildew. Temperature fluctuations can cause wood to expand and contract, leading to structural damage. These issues not only impact the aesthetics of your furniture but can also pose safety hazards. Ignoring these problems can lead to costly repairs down the line. For example, a seemingly minor crack in a chair leg can escalate into a complete structural failure. Sai Furniture provides expert architectural education programs to educate on preventative measures and proper furniture care.

Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's climate presents unique challenges for furniture. The high humidity can lead to warping, swelling, and mildew, while fluctuating temperatures can cause cracking and damage to various finishes. Common problems we encounter include:

Wood rot and decay: Caused by moisture and pests. Cracked and chipped finishes: Resulting from temperature changes and impacts. Loose joints and broken components: Due to wear and tear or accidental damage. Upholstery damage: Tears, stains, and general wear and tear.

These issues not only affect the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but can also pose safety risks. Ignoring these problems can lead to further damage and costly repairs down the line.

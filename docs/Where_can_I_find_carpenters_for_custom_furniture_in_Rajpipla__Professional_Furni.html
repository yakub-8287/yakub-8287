The Challenges of Furniture in Surat's Climate

Surat's climate, with its fluctuating humidity and temperatures, presents unique challenges for furniture. The high humidity can lead to warping, swelling, and cracking in poorly constructed pieces, while extreme temperatures can cause damage to finishes and materials. Common problems include:

Cracked or broken wooden furniture legs Damaged upholstery and fabric Sagging drawer bottoms Loose joints and fittings

These issues not only detract from the aesthetics of your home but can also significantly impact its functionality and value. Repairing or replacing damaged furniture can also be surprisingly costly. Finally, attempting DIY repairs often leads to further damage or safety hazards.

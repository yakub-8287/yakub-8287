2. Common Furniture Problems in Surat: Addressing the Challenges

Surat's humid climate and fluctuating temperatures can take a toll on your furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and warping: The high humidity can cause wood to absorb moisture, leading to warping, cracking, and even rot. Fading and discoloration: Exposure to sunlight can fade fabrics and finishes. Pest infestation: Certain insects thrive in humid conditions, damaging furniture. Loose joints and broken parts: Years of use can weaken joints and cause breakage.

These issues not only diminish the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but can also pose safety hazards. Ignoring these problems can lead to costly replacements down the line.

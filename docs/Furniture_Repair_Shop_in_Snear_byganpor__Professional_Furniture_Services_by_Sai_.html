2. Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's hot and humid climate presents unique challenges for furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and warping: Excessive humidity can cause wood to swell, crack, and rot, especially in older, untreated pieces. Insect infestation: Warm temperatures create ideal breeding grounds for wood-boring insects. Upholstery damage: Spills, stains, and general wear and tear can quickly compromise the appearance and comfort of upholstered furniture. Structural damage: Broken legs, loose joints, and damaged frames can render your furniture unsafe and unusable. Sun damage: Prolonged exposure to the sun can cause fading and cracking in wood and fabrics.

These problems not only affect the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but can also lead to costly replacements. Ignoring minor issues can escalate into major repairs, ultimately costing more in the long run. Furthermore, damaged furniture can pose safety risks, especially for children and the elderly.

Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's climate, with its high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, can take a toll on furniture. Common issues we address include:

Wood rot and decay: Excessive moisture leads to weakening and damage to wooden furniture. Cracked and damaged veneers: Temperature changes can cause veneers to crack or peel. Upholstery wear and tear: Daily use and exposure to the elements can damage fabric and cushions. Loose joints and broken legs: These are common problems that impact both functionality and aesthetics. Insect infestation: Certain wood-boring insects thrive in humid climates.

Ignoring these problems can lead to costly replacements and even safety hazards.

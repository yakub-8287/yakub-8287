Common Furniture Problems in Surat's Climate

Surat's humid climate and fluctuating temperatures can wreak havoc on your furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and warping: The high humidity can lead to wood expanding and potentially rotting, especially in older pieces. Pest infestation: Warm, humid conditions can attract termites and other wood-boring insects. Cracks and splits: Temperature fluctuations can cause wood to expand and contract, leading to cracks. Fading and discoloration: Sunlight can cause furniture to fade, particularly if it's not protected.

These issues not only impact the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but can also compromise its structural integrity, leading to costly replacements down the line.

2. Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's climate, with its fluctuating temperatures and high humidity, can significantly impact your furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and warping: Moisture damage is a major problem, causing wood to rot, warp, and crack. Pest infestation: Termites and other insects thrive in humid conditions, damaging furniture structures. Fading and discoloration: Sunlight and humidity can cause finishes to fade and discolor over time. Scratches and dents: Daily wear and tear inevitably leads to surface damage.

Repairing damaged furniture is often far more cost-effective than purchasing replacements, especially for valuable antique pieces. Ignoring these issues can lead to further, more expensive damage down the line, potentially compromising safety.

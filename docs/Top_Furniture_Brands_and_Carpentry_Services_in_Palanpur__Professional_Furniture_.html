Common Furniture Problems in Surat

The humid climate of Surat and fluctuating temperatures can significantly impact your furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and decay: Excessive moisture can lead to wood rot, weakening the structure and damaging the aesthetics of your furniture. Cracked or damaged wood: Changes in temperature and humidity can cause wood to expand and contract, leading to cracks and splits. Pest infestations: Warm, humid conditions can attract pests like termites and woodworms, causing significant damage. Upholstery wear and tear: Constant use and exposure to the elements can cause upholstery to fade, tear, or become stained.

These problems not only affect the appearance of your furniture but can also pose safety risks and lead to costly replacements.

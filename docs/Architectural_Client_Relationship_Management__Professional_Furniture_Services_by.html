Common Furniture Issues in Surat: A Problem Statement

Surat's climate, characterized by high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, presents unique challenges for furniture. The humid air can lead to warping, cracking, and the growth of mold and mildew, especially in wooden furniture. Exposure to intense sunlight can cause fading and deterioration. These issues not only affect the aesthetics but also compromise the structural integrity of your furniture, leading to costly repairs or replacements down the line. Neglecting these problems can result in safety hazards like unstable chairs or broken tables. We've seen firsthand the impact of these challenges on homes and businesses across Surat, near landmarks like the Dumas Beach and the textile market. Many traditional Surat furniture styles, like intricately carved wooden pieces, are particularly vulnerable to these issues.

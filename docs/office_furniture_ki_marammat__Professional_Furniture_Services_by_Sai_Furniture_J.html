Common Office Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's climate, with its high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, can significantly impact the lifespan of office furniture. Common problems we encounter include:

Metal corrosion: Exposure to moisture can cause metal components to rust and weaken. Damaged veneers: Veneers can peel or chip due to impact or age.

Common Furniture Problems in Surat's Climate

Surat's unique climate, characterized by high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, can significantly impact the lifespan and condition of your furniture. Common issues include:

Wood warping and cracking: Fluctuations in humidity cause wood to expand and contract, leading to warping, cracks, and structural damage. Pest infestations: Warm, humid conditions can attract termites and other wood-boring insects, causing significant damage to furniture. Paint peeling and fading: The intense sunlight and humidity can cause paint to peel, fade, and chip, detracting from the aesthetic appeal of your furniture. Loose joints and broken parts: Years of wear and tear, coupled with environmental stressors, can lead to loose joints and broken parts, compromising the structural integrity of your furniture. Neglecting these issues can lead to further damage and higher repair costs.

These problems not only affect the appearance of your furniture but can also pose safety risks. A damaged chair, for instance, could collapse under weight, causing injury.

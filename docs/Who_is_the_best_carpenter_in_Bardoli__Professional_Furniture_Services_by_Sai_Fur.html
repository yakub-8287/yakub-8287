Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's humid climate and fluctuating temperatures can wreak havoc on your furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and warping: Excess moisture can cause wood to rot and warp, compromising its structural integrity. Cracked and damaged surfaces: Temperature changes can lead to cracking and damage in various furniture pieces, particularly those made of solid wood. Pest infestations: Warm, humid conditions create ideal breeding grounds for wood-boring insects. Loose joints and broken components: Everyday wear and tear can weaken furniture, leading to structural instability.

These problems not only detract from the aesthetics of your home but can also pose safety risks, especially for older or poorly maintained pieces. Ignoring these issues can lead to more expensive repairs down the line.

Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's hot and humid climate poses significant challenges to furniture. Monsoonal rains and fluctuating temperatures often lead to:

Wood rot and warping: Moisture damage is a major concern affecting both wooden and cane furniture prevalent in Surat homes. Fabric damage: Sun exposure and humidity can lead to fading, mildew, and tears in upholstery. Pest infestations: Termites and other wood-boring insects are common issues requiring professional treatment. Structural damage: Accidents, wear and tear, or improper use can cause damage to the structure of your furniture.

Ignoring these issues can lead to costly repairs later on and may even compromise the safety of your furniture.

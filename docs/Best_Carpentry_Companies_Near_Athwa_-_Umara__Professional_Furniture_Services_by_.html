2. Problem Statement: Common Furniture Issues in Surat

Surat's humid climate and fluctuating temperatures can significantly damage furniture. Common problems include:

Wood rot and warping: Excess moisture leads to wood deterioration and structural instability, especially in older furniture pieces. Pest infestation: Termites and other insects thrive in humid conditions, compromising the integrity of wooden furniture. Cracks and splits: Temperature changes cause wood to expand and contract, leading to cracks and splits in furniture frames and surfaces. Upholstery damage: Humidity can cause mildew and mold growth in fabric upholstery, creating an unpleasant smell and potentially causing health problems. Loose joints and broken components: General wear and tear can result in unstable furniture and safety hazards.

Ignoring these issues can lead to costly replacements, or worse, accidents from unsafe furniture.

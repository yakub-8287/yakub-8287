2. The Challenges of Outdoor Furniture in Surat

Surat’s climate presents unique challenges for outdoor furniture. High humidity can lead to wood rot and metal corrosion, while intense sun can fade fabrics and crack finishes. Ignoring these issues can lead to costly repairs down the line, and even safety hazards. Many Surat residents struggle with:

Damaged Wooden Furniture: Sun-baked wood cracks, splinters, and becomes unstable. Rusting Metal Frames: Constant humidity causes rust and weakens structural integrity. Faded and Worn Fabrics: Intense sun and rain damage outdoor cushions and upholstery. Unsafe Structures: Deteriorated furniture poses a risk of injury, especially for children.

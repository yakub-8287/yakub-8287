Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's climate, with its fluctuating humidity and temperatures, can significantly impact wooden furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and decay: Excess moisture weakens the wood structure, leading to rotting and cracking. Cracks and splits: Changes in temperature cause expansion and contraction, resulting in cracks and splits in the wood. Scratches and dents: Everyday wear and tear can leave your furniture looking less-than-perfect. Loose joints and broken components: Years of use can cause joints to loosen or break completely. Pest infestation: Woodworm and other insects can damage the structure of your furniture, requiring professional treatment.

These problems not only diminish the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but can also compromise its structural integrity, leading to safety concerns and potentially costly replacements.

The Challenges of Maintaining Furniture in Surat's Climate

Surat's climate presents unique challenges for furniture. The high humidity and fluctuating temperatures can lead to various problems:

Wood warping and cracking: The intense heat and humidity can cause wooden furniture to warp, crack, and lose its structural integrity. Fabric deterioration: Dampness promotes mold and mildew growth on upholstery, compromising its appearance and durability. Pest infestations: Warm, humid conditions are ideal breeding grounds for wood-boring insects and other pests. High repair costs: Neglecting these issues can lead to costly repairs or even the need for complete furniture replacement.

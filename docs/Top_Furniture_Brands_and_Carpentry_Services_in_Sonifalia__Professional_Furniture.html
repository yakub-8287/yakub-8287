Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's climate presents unique challenges to furniture. High humidity can lead to wood rot, mold growth, and warping, particularly in older pieces. Fluctuating temperatures contribute to cracking and splitting in wood, while dust and wear-and-tear can diminish the aesthetic appeal of your furniture. These issues not only detract from the beauty of your home but can also pose safety hazards. Consider the cost of replacing damaged furniture versus the cost of professional repair – often, repair is significantly more economical and environmentally friendly.

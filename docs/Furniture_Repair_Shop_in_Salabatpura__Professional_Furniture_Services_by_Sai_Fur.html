2. Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's hot and humid climate can significantly impact the lifespan of your furniture. Common problems include:

Wood rot and decay: Excessive moisture can lead to wood damage, weakening the structure and causing unsightly blemishes. Cracked or damaged frames: Changes in temperature and humidity can cause wood to expand and contract, resulting in cracks and splits. Faded or stained upholstery: Sun exposure and dust can cause upholstery to fade or become stained. Loose joints and wobbly legs: Years of use can loosen joints and cause furniture to become unstable. Pest infestations: Termites and other pests can cause significant damage to wooden furniture.

These issues can be costly to fix, and ignoring them can lead to further damage and even safety hazards.

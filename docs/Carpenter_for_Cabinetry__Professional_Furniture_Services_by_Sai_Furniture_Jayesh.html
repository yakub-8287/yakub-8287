Common Furniture Problems in Surat: A Reality Check

Surat's humid climate and fluctuating temperatures can wreak havoc on your furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and warping: High humidity leads to wood expansion, causing warping, cracking, and even rot. Pest infestations: Warm, humid conditions create ideal breeding grounds for insects that can damage furniture. Fading and discoloration: Exposure to sunlight can cause fabrics and wood finishes to fade. Structural damage: Years of use and neglect can lead to loose joints, broken legs, and other structural issues.

These problems don't just affect aesthetics; they can also impact safety and lead to costly replacements. Ignoring minor issues can result in more extensive and expensive repairs down the line.

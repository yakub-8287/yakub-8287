Common Furniture Issues in Surat

Surat's climate, with its fluctuating humidity and temperatures, can significantly impact wooden furniture. Common problems include:

Cracks and splits: Caused by fluctuating humidity levels. Water damage: Leading to warping and rotting, especially during monsoon season. Scratches and dents: From everyday wear and tear. Pest infestation: A concern for older furniture pieces. Loose joints and broken parts: Affecting functionality and aesthetics.

These issues can not only affect the appearance of your furniture but also pose safety hazards. Neglecting repairs can lead to costly replacements down the line.

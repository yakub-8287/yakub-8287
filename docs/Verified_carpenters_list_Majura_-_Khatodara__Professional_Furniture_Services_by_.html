Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's humid climate and fluctuating temperatures can wreak havoc on your furniture. Common issues include:

Damaged upholstery: Sunlight and humidity can fade and damage fabric, requiring professional cleaning or replacement. Scratches and dents: Daily use leaves its mark, creating unsightly blemishes on your furniture.

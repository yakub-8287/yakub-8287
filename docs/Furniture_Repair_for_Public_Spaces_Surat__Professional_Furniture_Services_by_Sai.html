The Problem: Public Space Furniture Challenges in Surat

Surat, with its vibrant culture and dynamic lifestyle, presents unique challenges to maintaining public space furniture. The humid climate and fluctuating temperatures of Gujarat can lead to:

Wood rot and warping: Moisture damage is a significant issue, causing wood to decay and lose its structural integrity. Fabric wear and tear: Constant use and exposure to the elements quickly damage fabric upholstery. Metal corrosion: Rust and oxidation are common problems with outdoor metal furniture. Vandalism and accidental damage: Public spaces are susceptible to accidental damage and deliberate acts of vandalism. High replacement costs: Replacing damaged furniture is expensive, impacting budgets significantly.

These issues not only affect the aesthetics but also pose safety hazards to the public. Proactive furniture repair is crucial to mitigate these risks.

The Problem: Insect Damage to Your Furniture

Surat's humid climate, particularly during the monsoon season, creates the perfect breeding ground for various wood-boring insects like termites and woodworms. These pests can cause extensive damage to your wooden furniture, leading to structural weakening, unsightly holes, and even complete destruction. This isn't just an aesthetic issue; untreated infestations can lead to significant repair costs, and potentially compromise the safety and structural integrity of your furniture. Ignoring the problem can quickly escalate the damage, making the repair more costly and complex. Many Surat homes, with their beautiful traditional and modern furniture pieces, are susceptible to this type of damage.

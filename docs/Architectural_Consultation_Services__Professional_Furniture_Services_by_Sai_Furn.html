Common Furniture Issues in Surat: The Problem Statement

Surat's climate, with its high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, presents unique challenges for furniture. Common problems include:

Wood rot and warping: The intense humidity can cause wooden furniture to absorb moisture, leading to warping, cracking, and even rot. Pest infestations: Termites and other wood-boring insects thrive in Surat's warm climate, damaging furniture structures. Fabric fading and deterioration: Sunlight and humidity can cause fabric to fade, weaken, and become less durable. Metal corrosion: Exposure to moisture can lead to rust and corrosion in metal furniture components.

These issues not only detract from the aesthetic value of your furniture but also pose safety risks and can lead to costly replacements.

Common Furniture Issues in Surat: The Holiday Challenge

The humid Surat climate and frequent temperature fluctuations can significantly impact your furniture. Common problems include:

Wood rot and damage: Excess moisture can lead to warping, cracking, and even rotting, especially in older pieces. Loose joints and broken components: The constant shifting of furniture during celebrations can exacerbate pre-existing damage. Scratches and dents: Accidental bumps and scrapes are inevitable, particularly during busy holiday gatherings. Faded finishes: Exposure to sunlight and humidity can dull the vibrancy of your furniture's finish.

These issues not only affect the aesthetics of your furniture but can also pose safety risks, especially for antique or delicate pieces. Ignoring these problems can lead to more extensive and costly repairs in the long run.

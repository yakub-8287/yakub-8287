Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's humid climate and fluctuating temperatures can wreak havoc on your furniture. Common issues we address include:

Wood rot and damage: High humidity can lead to wood swelling, cracking, and even rot, especially in older pieces. Polishing and finish damage: Exposure to sunlight and dust can cause fading and scratches. Upholstery wear and tear: Regular use, coupled with humidity, can lead to fabric deterioration. Structural issues: Loose joints, broken legs, or damaged frames are common problems affecting both traditional and modern furniture. Pest infestation: Termites and other insects can cause significant damage if left untreated.

Ignoring these problems can lead to further damage, increasing repair costs and potentially compromising the safety of your furniture.

Common Furniture Problems in Surat & Vapi

Surat and Vapi, with their humid climate and fluctuating temperatures, present unique challenges for furniture. Common problems include:

Wood rot and warping: High humidity can cause wood to absorb moisture, leading to warping, cracking, and rot, particularly affecting furniture made from less durable woods. Pest infestation: Certain types of insects thrive in humid environments, causing damage to wooden furniture. Fading and discoloration: Exposure to direct sunlight can cause furniture to fade and lose its original vibrancy. Mechanical damage: Daily wear and tear, accidental impacts, and general usage contribute to furniture damage.

These issues not only detract from the aesthetic appeal of your home but can also pose safety hazards and lead to costly replacements.

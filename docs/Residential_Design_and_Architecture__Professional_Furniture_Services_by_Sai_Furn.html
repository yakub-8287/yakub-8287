The Challenges of Maintaining Furniture in Surat's Climate

Surat’s humid climate and fluctuating temperatures present unique challenges for furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and warping: High humidity can lead to wood damage, affecting the structural integrity and aesthetic appeal of your furniture. Pest infestation: Warm, humid conditions can attract insects and other pests, damaging your valuable pieces. Fabric fading and deterioration: Sun exposure and humidity can cause fabric to fade, tear, and lose its shape. Metal rust and corrosion: Changes in temperature and humidity accelerate the deterioration of metal furniture.

These problems not only affect the appearance of your furniture but can also result in costly repairs or replacements.

Common Furniture Problems in Surat's Climate

Surat's hot and humid climate presents unique challenges for furniture. High humidity can lead to warping, swelling, and mold growth, especially in wooden pieces. Extreme temperatures can cause cracking and fading. These issues can not only detract from the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but also compromise its structural integrity and safety. Ignoring these problems can lead to costly replacements down the line. Furthermore, damaged furniture can pose safety risks, especially if it's unstable or has sharp splinters.

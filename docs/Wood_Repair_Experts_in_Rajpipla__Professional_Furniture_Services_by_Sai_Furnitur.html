Common Furniture Problems in Surat

The humid and often variable climate of Surat presents significant challenges for wooden furniture. Exposure to fluctuating temperatures and high humidity can lead to:

Cracking and warping: Changes in moisture levels cause wood to expand and contract, leading to cracks and distortion. Pest infestation: Certain insects thrive in damp conditions and can infest wooden furniture, causing structural damage. Fading and discoloration: Sun exposure and humidity can fade the finish and discolor the wood. Scratches and dents: Daily wear and tear are inevitable, leaving unsightly marks on cherished furniture.

These problems not only diminish the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but can also compromise its structural integrity and safety, leading to costly repairs down the line.

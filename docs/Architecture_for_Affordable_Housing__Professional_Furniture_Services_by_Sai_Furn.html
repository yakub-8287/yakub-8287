The Problem with Furniture in Affordable Housing in Surat

Many affordable housing projects in Surat face the common issue of furniture quickly deteriorating due to the region's humid climate and extreme temperatures. This leads to:

Frequent repairs or replacements: This can be a significant financial burden for residents, negating the cost savings of affordable housing. Safety hazards: Damaged furniture can pose safety risks, especially for children. Aesthetic degradation: Poor-quality or damaged furniture detracts from the overall quality of life within the home. Material breakdown: The humid Surat climate accelerates the deterioration of wood and other materials commonly used in affordable housing furniture.

For example, the traditional khatla (wooden bed) common in many Surat homes may warp or become infested with insects if not properly maintained. The intense summer heat can cause fabrics to fade and crack.

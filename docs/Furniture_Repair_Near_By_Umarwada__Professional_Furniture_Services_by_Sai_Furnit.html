Common Furniture Problems in Surat: The Challenges of Our Climate

Surat's humid climate and fluctuating temperatures can significantly impact your furniture. Common issues include:

Insect infestation: Warm, humid conditions create a breeding ground for termites and wood-boring insects, damaging the structural integrity of your furniture.

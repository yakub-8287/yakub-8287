Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's climate, with its high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, can significantly impact the lifespan of your furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and warping: High humidity can cause wood to swell, crack, and rot, especially in older pieces. Pest infestations: Warm weather creates a breeding ground for termites and other wood-boring insects. Fabric fading and damage: Sunlight and humidity can fade fabrics and damage upholstery. Joint failure: Changes in temperature and humidity can weaken joints, causing furniture to become unstable.

These problems can lead to costly repairs or even the need to replace furniture prematurely.

2. Common Furniture Problems in Surat: The Challenges

Surat's hot and humid climate presents unique challenges for furniture. Common problems include:

Wood rot and warping: Excessive moisture can cause wood to expand, crack, and rot, particularly in older pieces. Pest infestation: Termites and other insects thrive in warm, humid environments, damaging wooden furniture. Sun damage: Prolonged exposure to sunlight can fade finishes and weaken wood. Loose joints and broken parts: Years of use can take their toll, resulting in wobbly chairs, broken legs, and loose joints.

These problems not only affect the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but can also pose safety risks. Ignoring these issues can lead to costly repairs down the line, and even dangerous situations.

Common Furniture Issues in Surat

Surat's climate, with its fluctuating humidity and temperatures, presents unique challenges for wooden furniture. Common problems include:

Water damage: Monsoon season can cause significant damage, leading to warping, swelling, and cracking. Termite infestation: A persistent concern requiring specialized treatment. Scratches and dents: From daily wear and tear or accidental bumps. Loose joints and broken legs: Affecting both stability and aesthetics. Fading and discoloration: Due to sun exposure and age.

These issues not only detract from the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but can also impact its structural integrity and safety, potentially leading to expensive replacements.

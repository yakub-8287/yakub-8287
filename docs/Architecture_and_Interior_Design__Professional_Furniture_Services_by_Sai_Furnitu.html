Common Furniture Problems in Surat's Climate

Surat's hot and humid climate presents unique challenges for furniture. High humidity can lead to wood warping, mildew growth, and fabric deterioration. Extreme temperatures can cause cracking and splitting in wooden pieces, and prolonged sun exposure can fade fabrics and finishes. These problems not only affect the aesthetics of your furniture but can also pose safety risks, such as unstable structures or the release of harmful mold spores. Repairing these issues can be costly, impacting your budget significantly. Ignoring these problems can lead to premature furniture replacement, adding more expense in the long run.

Common Furniture Problems in Surat

The humid climate of Surat, along with fluctuating temperatures, can significantly affect the longevity and condition of your furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and warping: Excessive moisture leads to wood damage. Pest infestation: Warm, humid conditions attract termites and other pests. Fabric damage: Sun exposure and humidity can cause fabric fading and deterioration. Joint failure: Changes in temperature and humidity can loosen joints.

These problems not only detract from the aesthetics of your furniture but can also lead to costly repairs or even complete replacement down the line.

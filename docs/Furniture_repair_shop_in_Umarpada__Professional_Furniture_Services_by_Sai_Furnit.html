2. Common Furniture Problems in Surat & Their Solutions

The humid climate of Surat, coupled with fluctuating temperatures, can cause significant damage to your furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and warping: The high humidity encourages the growth of mold and fungi, weakening wood structures and causing warping. Cracked or broken joints: Fluctuating temperatures can cause wood to expand and contract, leading to stress fractures and broken joints. Faded finishes: Exposure to sunlight and humidity can cause the finish on your furniture to fade and lose its luster. Pest infestation: Certain insects are attracted to wood, causing further damage and compromising the structural integrity of your furniture.

Ignoring these issues can lead to costly replacements or even safety hazards. Don't let a minor problem escalate into a major expense.

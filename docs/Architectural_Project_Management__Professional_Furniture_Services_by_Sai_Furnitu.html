Common Furniture Issues in Surat's Climate

Surat's humid climate and fluctuating temperatures pose unique challenges for furniture. Exposure to monsoon rains can lead to warping, rot, and pest infestations. High humidity can cause mildew and discoloration, especially in traditional wooden furniture. These issues not only affect the aesthetic appeal but also compromise the structural integrity and safety of your furniture, leading to costly repairs or replacements down the line. Ignoring these problems can also create safety hazards, particularly with weakened structures.

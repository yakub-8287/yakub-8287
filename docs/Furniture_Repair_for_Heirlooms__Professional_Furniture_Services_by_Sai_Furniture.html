Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's climate, with its high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, poses unique challenges to furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and decay: Moisture can damage the structural integrity of wooden furniture. Cracked or damaged veneers: Changes in temperature and humidity can cause veneers to separate or crack. Insect infestation: Certain insects thrive in warm, humid conditions and can cause significant damage. Loose joints and structural instability: Years of use can weaken joints, causing instability. Damaged upholstery and finishes: Wear and tear can lead to fading, staining, and deterioration of fabrics and finishes. This is especially true for traditional Gujarati furniture styles.

Ignoring these problems can lead to irreversible damage and costly repairs down the line. Furthermore, damaged furniture can pose safety risks, especially if structural integrity is compromised.

2. Problem Statement: Common Dining Furniture Issues in Surat

The hot and humid Surat climate takes a toll on wooden furniture. Common problems include:

Cracked or warped wood: Fluctuations in temperature and humidity can cause wood to expand and contract, leading to cracks and warping. Loose joints and wobbly legs: Years of use can loosen joints, making tables and chairs unstable and potentially unsafe. Scratches and stains: Daily wear and tear, especially with young children, often leads to unsightly damage. Damaged upholstery: Fabrics can fade, tear, or become stained over time, detracting from the overall appearance.

Ignoring these issues can lead to further damage, increased repair costs, and even safety hazards. Don't wait until your furniture is beyond repair—act now!

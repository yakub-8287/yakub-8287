Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's climate, with its fluctuating humidity and temperatures, presents unique challenges for furniture. The intense heat can cause wood to dry out and crack, while high humidity can lead to warping and mold growth. These issues are particularly common in traditional wooden furniture, prevalent in many Surat homes. Ignoring these problems can lead to costly repairs down the line, even resulting in the complete loss of cherished family heirlooms. Further, attempting DIY repairs can often cause more harm than good.

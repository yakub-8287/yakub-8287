Common Furniture Problems in Surat & Their Solutions

Surat's unique climate, with its fluctuating humidity and temperatures, poses significant challenges to wooden furniture. Common issues include:

Cracks and Splits: Extreme temperature changes can cause wood to expand and contract, leading to unsightly cracks. Water Damage: Spills and leaks can cause significant damage, weakening the wood structure. Scratches and Dents: Daily wear and tear can leave your furniture looking worn. Pest Infestation: Wood-boring insects are a common problem in humid environments. Loose Joints and Dowels: Over time, joints can loosen, compromising the stability of your furniture.

Ignoring these issues can lead to costly replacements down the line, so addressing them promptly is crucial.

2. Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's climate, with its fluctuating humidity and temperature, presents unique challenges for wooden furniture. Common problems include:

Cracks and Splits: Extreme temperature changes cause wood to expand and contract, leading to unsightly cracks and splits. Water Damage: Spills and unexpected moisture can cause significant damage, leading to warping, rotting, and mold. Scratches and Dents: Daily wear and tear inevitably leaves its mark on furniture, diminishing its aesthetic appeal. Pest Infestation: Certain insects can bore into wood, weakening its structure and potentially damaging its integrity. Loose Joints and Broken Parts: Over time, furniture joints can loosen, leading to instability and structural issues.

Ignoring these problems can lead to increased repair costs in the long run and, in severe cases, irreparable damage. The safety of your family is also important, and weakened furniture poses safety concerns, particularly with children present.

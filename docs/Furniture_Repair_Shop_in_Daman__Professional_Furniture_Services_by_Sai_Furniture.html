Common Furniture Problems in Surat: The Challenges We Address

Surat's climate, with its fluctuating humidity and temperatures, presents unique challenges for furniture. Common problems include:

Wood rot and warping: The high humidity can cause wood to swell, crack, and rot, especially in older pieces. Upholstery damage: Tears, stains, and general wear and tear are common issues, impacting both comfort and aesthetics. Loose joints and broken legs: Years of use can weaken joints, leading to instability and potential accidents. Pest infestation: Certain pests thrive in humid conditions, damaging wooden furniture.

These issues not only impact the aesthetics of your home but can also pose safety hazards and lead to costly replacements.

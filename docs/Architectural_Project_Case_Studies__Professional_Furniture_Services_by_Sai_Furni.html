Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's humid climate and fluctuating temperatures present unique challenges for furniture. The combination of heat and moisture can lead to:

Wood rot and decay: Especially prevalent in older, untreated furniture. Warping and cracking: Changes in humidity can cause wood to expand and contract, leading to damage. Pest infestation: Warm, humid conditions can attract insects and other pests. Fabric deterioration: High humidity can cause fabrics to mildew and fade.

These issues not only affect the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but can also pose safety concerns and lead to costly replacements.

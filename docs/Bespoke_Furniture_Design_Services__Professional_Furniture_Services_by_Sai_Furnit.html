The Challenges of Furniture in Surat: A Problem Statement

Surat's hot and humid climate presents unique challenges for furniture. High humidity can lead to warping, mildew, and insect infestation, while fluctuating temperatures can cause expansion and contraction, damaging even the sturdiest pieces. Finding quality, long-lasting furniture can be difficult, often involving high upfront costs and potential safety risks with poorly constructed items. Many older pieces may require extensive furniture repair or furniture restoration to extend their lifespan and maintain their value.

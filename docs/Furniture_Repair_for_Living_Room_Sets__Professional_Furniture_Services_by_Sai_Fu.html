Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's climate, with its fluctuating humidity and temperatures, can take a toll on your furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and warping: The high humidity can cause wood to swell and crack. Fabric wear and tear: Sunlight and constant use can fade and damage upholstery. Loose joints and broken components: Over time, furniture components can loosen or break due to regular use. Pest infestation: Certain insects can damage wooden frames and upholstery. Scratches and water damage: Accidental damage can compromise both aesthetics and structural integrity.

These problems not only impact the appearance of your living room but can also pose safety hazards. Neglecting these issues can lead to more expensive repairs down the line.

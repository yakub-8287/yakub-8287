2. Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's climate, with its fluctuating humidity and temperatures, can wreak havoc on wooden furniture. Common problems include:

Cracks and Splits: Fluctuating humidity causes wood to expand and contract, leading to cracks and splits. Water Damage: Spills and accidental exposure to moisture can cause significant damage. Scratches and Dents: Everyday wear and tear can leave your furniture looking less than its best. Pest Infestation: Woodworm and other pests can weaken the structure of your furniture. Loose Joints: Years of use can cause joints to loosen, leading to instability.

These issues not only affect the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but can also pose safety risks and lead to costly replacements.

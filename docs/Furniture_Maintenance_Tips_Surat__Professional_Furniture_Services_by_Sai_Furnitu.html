2. Problem Statement: Common Furniture Issues in Surat's Climate

Surat's climate, characterized by high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, can significantly impact your furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and warping: Excess moisture can cause wood to swell, crack, and even rot. Fading and discoloration: Sunlight exposure, especially during the intense summer months, can lead to furniture fading. Pest infestations: Warm and humid conditions can attract insects like termites and borers. Upholstery damage: Spills, stains, and general wear and tear can quickly diminish the beauty of your upholstered furniture.

These problems not only affect the aesthetic appeal but can also lead to costly repairs or even complete replacement if left unaddressed. Ignoring these issues can also pose safety hazards.

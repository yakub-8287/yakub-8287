Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat, with its humid climate and fluctuating temperatures, presents unique challenges for furniture. Common problems include:

Wood rot and warping: The high humidity can lead to wood deterioration and structural damage, especially in older furniture. Pest infestation: Termites and other pests are a significant concern, requiring professional treatment and repair. Cracks and splits: Sudden temperature changes can cause wood to expand and contract, leading to cracks and splits in furniture. Loose joints and broken parts: Everyday wear and tear can result in structural instability and safety hazards. Outdated styles: You may wish to update the style of your existing furniture to better suit your current decor.

These issues not only detract from the aesthetic appeal of your home but can also pose safety risks and lead to costly replacements.

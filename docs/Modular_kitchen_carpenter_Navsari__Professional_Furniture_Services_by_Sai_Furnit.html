2. Common Furniture Problems in Surat's Climate

Surat's humid climate and fluctuating temperatures can significantly impact your furniture. Common problems include:

Wood warping and cracking: Humidity causes wood to expand and contract, leading to damage. Pest infestations: Warm, humid conditions create ideal breeding grounds for termites and other pests. Fading and discoloration: Sunlight can fade the color of your furniture over time. Hardware failure: Repeated exposure to moisture can cause hinges, handles, and other hardware to rust or malfunction.

Ignoring these issues can lead to costly repairs down the line and even compromise the safety of your family. Investing in professional furniture care is crucial for long-term value and peace of mind.

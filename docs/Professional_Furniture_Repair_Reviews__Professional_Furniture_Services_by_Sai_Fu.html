Common Furniture Problems in Surat & Their Solutions

Surat's hot and humid climate poses several challenges to furniture. High humidity leads to wood swelling, warping, and the growth of mold and mildew. Fluctuating temperatures can cause cracks and splits in wood, while dust and general wear and tear take their toll. These problems not only diminish the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but can also lead to costly repairs if left untreated. Ignoring these issues can also create safety hazards, such as unstable chairs or drawers.

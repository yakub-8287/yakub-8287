Common Furniture Problems in Surat's Climate

Surat's climate, characterized by high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, can significantly affect your furniture. Common problems include:

Fading and discoloration: Sunlight can cause furniture to fade and lose its vibrancy. Wear and tear: Daily use inevitably leads to wear and tear on your furniture.

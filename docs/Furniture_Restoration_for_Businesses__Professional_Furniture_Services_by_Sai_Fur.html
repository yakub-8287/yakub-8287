Common Furniture Problems Facing Businesses in Surat

The hot and humid climate of Surat, coupled with heavy usage, often leads to significant wear and tear on office furniture. Common problems include:

Wood rot and damage: The high humidity can cause wood to warp, crack, and rot, particularly in older pieces. Upholstery wear: Frequent use can lead to tears, stains, and fading in upholstery fabrics. Structural damage: Chairs may become wobbly, drawers may stick, and joints may loosen over time. Scratches and dents: Everyday wear and tear can result in cosmetic damage that detracts from the overall look of your furniture. Pest infestation: Certain pests thrive in humid conditions and can cause significant damage to furniture.

Ignoring these issues can lead to costly replacements, safety hazards, and a diminished professional image.

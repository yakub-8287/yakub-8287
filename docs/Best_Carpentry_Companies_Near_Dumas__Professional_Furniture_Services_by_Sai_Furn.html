Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's hot and humid climate presents unique challenges for furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and warping: The combination of heat and moisture can cause wood to expand, contract, and eventually rot. Pest infestation: Termites and other insects thrive in humid conditions, damaging wooden furniture. Sun damage: Prolonged sun exposure can fade finishes and crack the wood. Accidental damage: Everyday wear and tear, scratches, and broken parts are common.

These problems not only diminish the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but also pose safety risks and can lead to costly replacements.

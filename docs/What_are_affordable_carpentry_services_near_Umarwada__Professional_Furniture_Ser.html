Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat, with its vibrant climate, presents unique challenges for furniture. The high humidity and fluctuating temperatures can cause wood to warp, crack, or become infested with pests. These problems often lead to costly repairs or even complete furniture replacement. Common issues include:

Cracked or damaged wooden frames Loose joints and wobbly legs Scratches and water damage Pest infestations (termites, borers) Outdated or damaged finishes

Ignoring these problems can lead to further damage and significantly increase repair costs in the long run. Furthermore, damaged furniture can pose safety risks, especially if there are children or elderly individuals in your home.

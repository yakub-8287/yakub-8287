The Problem: Furniture Damage in Surat

Surat's unique climate, with its high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, can significantly impact the condition of your furniture. Wood can warp, upholstery can fade, and delicate finishes can crack. Common issues we address include:

Scratches and gouges: From everyday wear and tear. Damaged upholstery: Tears, stains, and fabric deterioration. Loose joints and broken legs: Common in older furniture. Water damage: From spills or leaks. Wood rot and insect infestation: Especially prevalent in humid conditions.

Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's unique climate, characterized by high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, presents significant challenges for furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and warping: Excessive moisture can lead to wood decay and structural damage, especially in older pieces. Upholstery damage: Humidity can contribute to mold and mildew growth in upholstery, causing discoloration and unpleasant odors. Insect infestations: Certain insects thrive in warm, humid conditions, potentially damaging both wood and fabric. Accidental damage: Scratches, dents, and tears are common occurrences, requiring professional repair.

These problems not only diminish the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but can also significantly impact its lifespan and even pose safety risks. Ignoring damage can lead to more extensive and costly repairs down the line.

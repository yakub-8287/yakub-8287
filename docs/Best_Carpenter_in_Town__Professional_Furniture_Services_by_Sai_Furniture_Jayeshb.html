Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's climate, with its fluctuating humidity and temperatures, presents unique challenges to furniture. Common issues include:

Wood warping and cracking: The intense heat and humidity can cause wood to expand and contract, leading to cracks and warping. Pest infestation: Termites and other pests thrive in humid conditions, damaging furniture structures. Faded finishes: Sunlight and humidity can cause finishes to fade and deteriorate over time. Loose joints and broken parts: General wear and tear, especially in high-traffic areas, can lead to structural damage.

These problems can not only affect the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but also pose safety hazards and lead to costly replacements.

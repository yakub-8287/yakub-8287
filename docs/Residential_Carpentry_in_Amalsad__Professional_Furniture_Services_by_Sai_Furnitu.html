Common Furniture Problems in Surat & Their Solutions

Surat's climate, characterized by high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, can significantly impact the lifespan of your furniture. Common problems include:

Wood warping and cracking: The extreme heat and humidity can cause wood to expand and contract, leading to cracks and warping. Pest infestations: Termites and other wood-boring insects thrive in humid conditions, potentially causing extensive damage. Loose joints and broken parts: Regular use and wear and tear can loosen joints or cause damage to delicate parts. Outdated styles and finishes: Your furniture may no longer complement your home's aesthetic.

Ignoring these issues not only impacts the aesthetics of your home but can also pose safety risks. Damaged furniture can become unstable and lead to accidents.

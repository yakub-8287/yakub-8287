2. Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's humid climate and fluctuating temperatures pose significant challenges for furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and warping: Excess moisture can lead to wood damage, affecting the structural integrity and aesthetics of your furniture. Pest infestation: Certain insects thrive in humid conditions, damaging wooden furniture. Cracked or broken joints: Age and wear and tear can weaken joints, leading to instability. Faded finishes: Sunlight and humidity can cause furniture finishes to fade and lose their luster. Upholstery damage: Tears, stains, and wear-and-tear on upholstered pieces are also common problems.

Ignoring these issues can result in costly replacements and potential safety hazards.

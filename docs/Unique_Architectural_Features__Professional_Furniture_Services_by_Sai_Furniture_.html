2. Common Furniture Problems in Surat: A Climate of Challenges

Surat's humid climate and fluctuating temperatures present unique challenges for furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and warping: The high humidity can cause wood to absorb moisture, leading to rotting, warping, and structural damage. Pest infestations: Warm temperatures create ideal breeding grounds for wood-boring insects, impacting the structural integrity and appearance of your furniture. Fabric fading and damage: The intense sunlight can cause fabric to fade and weaken, requiring professional cleaning and repair. Metal corrosion: High humidity accelerates the corrosion of metal furniture components.

Ignoring these problems can lead to costly repairs down the line and may even compromise safety.

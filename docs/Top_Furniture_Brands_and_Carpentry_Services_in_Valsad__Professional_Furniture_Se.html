Common Furniture Problems in Surat & Valsad

Surat, like many parts of Gujarat, experiences significant humidity and temperature fluctuations. This can lead to several common furniture issues:

Wood warping and cracking: Changes in humidity can cause wood to expand and contract, leading to cracks and warping. Weakened joints and structures: The constant shifts in temperature and humidity weaken the glue and joints in your furniture. Pest infestations: The climate can create ideal breeding grounds for wood-boring insects. Faded finishes: Sun exposure and humidity can cause your furniture's finish to fade and dull.

These problems not only affect the aesthetics of your furniture but can also compromise its safety and longevity, potentially leading to costly replacements.

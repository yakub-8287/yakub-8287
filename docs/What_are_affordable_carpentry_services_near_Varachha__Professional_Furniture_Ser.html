Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's climate, characterized by high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, presents unique challenges for furniture. Common problems include:

Wood rot and warping: Excessive humidity can lead to wood rot, warping, and cracking, especially in older furniture pieces. Insect infestation: Warm, humid conditions can attract termites and other wood-boring insects, causing significant damage. Loose joints and broken parts: Regular use and age can weaken joints and lead to breakage, impacting functionality and aesthetics. Faded finishes: Sun exposure and humidity can cause furniture finishes to fade and become dull.

These issues not only affect the appearance of your furniture but can also pose safety hazards. Ignoring these problems can lead to more extensive and costly repairs in the future.

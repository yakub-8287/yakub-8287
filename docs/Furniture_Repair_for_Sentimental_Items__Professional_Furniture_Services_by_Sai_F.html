Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat, with its unique climate, presents specific challenges to furniture. The high humidity and fluctuating temperatures can lead to various issues, including:

Wood rot and warping: The humid Surat weather can cause wood to absorb moisture, leading to decay and distortion. Cracks and splits: Changes in temperature can stress wood, causing it to crack. Pest infestation: Certain insects thrive in humid conditions and can damage your furniture. Upholstery wear and tear: Regular use and exposure to dust can damage the upholstery of your chairs and sofas. Loose joints and broken legs: Years of use can weaken joints and cause structural damage.

Addressing these issues promptly is essential to prevent further damage and potentially costly repairs. Neglecting these problems can lead to irreparable harm and the potential loss of cherished family heirlooms.

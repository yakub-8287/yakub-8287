Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's humid climate and fluctuating temperatures pose significant challenges to furniture. Common problems include:

Wood rot and warping: The high humidity can cause wood to swell, crack, and rot, especially in older pieces. Pest infestations: Termites and other insects are a common concern in Surat, damaging wooden furniture. Fabric damage: The heat and humidity can fade fabrics and cause deterioration. Loose joints and broken components: Frequent shifting or accidental damage can compromise furniture's structural integrity.

These issues not only impact the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but can also pose safety risks and costly repairs. Neglecting these issues can lead to further damage and ultimately higher repair costs.

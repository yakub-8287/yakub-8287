Common Furniture Problems in Surat Storage Units

Surat's unique climate, characterized by high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, can significantly impact stored furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and warping: Excess moisture leads to wood damage, often resulting in cracks, warping, and structural weakening. Pest infestation: Stored furniture can attract insects like termites and borers, leading to extensive damage. Scratching and chipping: Improper handling during storage or movement can lead to surface damage. Fabric deterioration: Moisture and lack of air circulation can damage upholstery and fabrics.

Ignoring these problems can lead to costly replacements and potential safety hazards. Addressing issues early with professional repair is significantly more cost-effective than buying new furniture.

2. Common Furniture Problems in Surat: The Challenges

Surat's hot and humid climate presents unique challenges for furniture. These issues can not only impact the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but also significantly affect its structural integrity and safety. Ignoring these problems can lead to costly replacements and potential safety hazards. For instance, a weakened chair frame could pose a risk of collapse, while damaged upholstery can harbor dust mites and allergens.

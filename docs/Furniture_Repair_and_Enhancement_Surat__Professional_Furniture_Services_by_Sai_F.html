2. Common Furniture Problems in Surat: Understanding the Challenges

Surat's climate, characterized by high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, poses significant challenges to furniture. Common problems include:

Wood rot and warping: Excess moisture can cause wood to swell, crack, and decay, particularly in older pieces. Fading and discoloration: Sunlight and humidity can cause fabrics and finishes to fade, losing their vibrancy. Pest infestation: Warm, humid conditions can attract insects that damage wood and upholstery. Structural damage: Accidental damage or years of wear and tear can compromise the structural integrity of your furniture.

These issues can be costly to repair if neglected, leading to the need for expensive replacements. Ignoring these problems can also present safety hazards, like unstable chairs or splintered wood.

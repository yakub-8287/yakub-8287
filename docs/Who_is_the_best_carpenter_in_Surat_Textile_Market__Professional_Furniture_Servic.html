The Furniture Problems Faced in Surat's Humid Climate

Surat's hot and humid climate presents unique challenges for furniture. The intense heat and moisture can cause wood to warp, crack, and become susceptible to pest infestation. Common issues include:

Wood rot and fungal growth: High humidity encourages mold and mildew, damaging the structural integrity of your furniture. Cracked and warped wood: Fluctuations in temperature and humidity cause wood to expand and contract, leading to cracks and warping. Loose joints and broken components: Years of use, combined with environmental factors, can weaken furniture joints and cause breakage. Pest infestation: Warm, humid conditions can attract termites and other wood-boring insects, leading to significant damage.

Ignoring these problems can lead to costly repairs down the line, potentially even requiring complete furniture replacement. Safety concerns can also arise from unstable or damaged furniture.

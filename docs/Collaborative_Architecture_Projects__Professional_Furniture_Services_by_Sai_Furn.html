The Challenges of Furniture in Surat's Climate

Surat, with its humid climate and fluctuating temperatures, presents unique challenges for furniture. The intense heat and humidity can warp wood, damage fabrics, and cause metal to rust. These issues not only diminish the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but also compromise its structural integrity and longevity. Ignoring these problems can lead to costly repairs down the line, compromising safety, and even potentially creating health hazards like pest infestations. Many older buildings in Surat, particularly those near the Tapi River, are particularly susceptible to these issues. Common problems include cracked wooden furniture, faded upholstery, and damaged joinery.

Common Furniture Issues in Surat & Their Implications

Surat's climate, characterized by high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, presents unique challenges for furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and decay: Excessive moisture can lead to structural damage and unsightly warping, particularly in older pieces. Pest infestations: Termites and other insects thrive in humid conditions, causing significant damage to furniture. Finish deterioration: Sunlight and temperature changes can fade or crack the finish, diminishing the aesthetic appeal. Loose joints and structural damage: Years of use can weaken joints and cause structural instability.

Ignoring these problems can lead to further deterioration, significantly impacting the value of your collectible furniture and potentially compromising safety. Repairing these issues promptly is crucial to preserving your investment.

2. Problem Statement: Common Outdoor Furniture Challenges in Surat

Surat's climate presents unique challenges for outdoor furniture. The intense heat can cause warping and cracking in wood, while humidity leads to mold and mildew growth. Frequent dust storms can scratch and dull finishes. These problems not only impact the aesthetics of your furniture but can also pose safety risks, like splintering wood or unstable structures. Ignoring these issues can lead to costly replacements down the line. For example, a seemingly minor crack in a wooden chair can quickly escalate into significant damage if left untreated.

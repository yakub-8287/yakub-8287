2. Common Furniture Problems in Surat: The Impact of Climate and Time

Surat's climate, with its fluctuating humidity and temperatures, presents unique challenges for wooden furniture. Common issues include:

Cracks and splits: Caused by changes in humidity levels, leading to structural weakness. Water damage: From spills or monsoon season leaks, resulting in warping and rot. Scratches and scuffs: From daily wear and tear, impacting the aesthetic appeal. Pest infestation: Termites and other wood-boring insects can cause significant damage. Loose joints and broken parts: Wear and tear, especially in older pieces.

These problems can lead to expensive repairs if not addressed promptly, or, worse, complete replacement. Ignoring these issues could also create safety hazards, especially if the structural integrity of the furniture is compromised.

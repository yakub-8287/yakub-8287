Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's climate, with its fluctuating humidity and temperatures, presents unique challenges for furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and warping: Excessive humidity can cause wood to expand and crack, leading to structural damage. Pest infestations: Termites and other insects thrive in humid conditions, damaging furniture frames and upholstery. Fading and discoloration: Direct sunlight can fade the color of your furniture, reducing its aesthetic appeal. Loose joints and broken components: General wear and tear, especially with older pieces, can lead to instability and safety concerns. These problems can be expensive to ignore, leading to costly replacements rather than manageable repairs.

Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's climate, characterized by high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, can significantly impact the lifespan of your furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and warping: The humidity can cause wood to expand and contract, leading to warping, cracks, and even rot. Pest infestation: Warm temperatures create ideal breeding grounds for termites and other wood-boring insects. Loose joints and broken parts: Daily wear and tear can weaken joints and cause parts to break, especially in older furniture. Damaged finishes: Scratches, stains, and peeling varnish are common problems affecting the aesthetic appeal of your furniture.

Ignoring these issues can lead to costly repairs down the line, and in some cases, complete furniture replacement. Furthermore, damaged furniture can pose safety hazards, especially if it involves sharp edges or unstable structures.

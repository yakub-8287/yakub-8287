Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's unique climate, characterized by high humidity and temperature fluctuations, can significantly impact furniture. Common problems include:

Wood warping and cracking: Changes in humidity can cause wooden furniture to warp, crack, or split. Pest infestation: Warm, humid conditions create the perfect environment for termites and other wood-boring insects. Upholstery damage: Sun exposure and humidity can fade fabrics and damage upholstery. Structural damage: Years of use can lead to loose joints, broken legs, or other structural issues.

These issues not only affect the aesthetics of your furniture but can also lead to costly repairs down the line. Ignoring these problems can compromise safety and the overall value of your investment.

Common Furniture Problems in Surat: The Challenges of Surat's Climate

Surat's climate, with its fluctuating humidity and temperatures, can significantly impact furniture. Common problems include:

Wood rot and warping: High humidity can lead to wood expansion and eventual damage, particularly affecting wooden furniture made from certain timbers. Cracks and splits: Extreme temperature changes can cause wood to shrink and crack, affecting the structural integrity of chairs, tables, and other pieces. Pest infestation: Certain insects thrive in humid conditions and can infest furniture, causing significant damage if left untreated. Upholstery wear and tear: Frequent use and the effects of sunlight and dust can damage upholstery, fading colors and weakening fabric.

Ignoring these issues can lead to costly replacements and potential safety hazards.

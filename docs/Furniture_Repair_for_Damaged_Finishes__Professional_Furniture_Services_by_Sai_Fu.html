Common Furniture Issues in Surat and Their Impact

Surat's climate, with its fluctuating humidity and temperatures, can take a toll on furniture. Exposure to sunlight can lead to fading and cracking, while moisture can cause swelling, warping, and mold. These issues aren't just cosmetic; they can compromise the structural integrity of your pieces, potentially making them unsafe. For example, a damaged chair leg could lead to an accident, while water damage in your intricately carved wooden chest might lead to irreparable damage. The cost of replacing damaged furniture can be substantial, making professional furniture repair a far more economical solution.

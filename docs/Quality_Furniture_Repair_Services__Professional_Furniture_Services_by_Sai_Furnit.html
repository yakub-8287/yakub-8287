2. The Problem: Common Furniture Issues in Surat

Surat's climate, with its fluctuations in temperature and humidity, presents unique challenges for furniture. Common problems include:

Wood rot and warping: High humidity can cause wood to absorb moisture, leading to rotting and warping, especially in pieces made of less durable woods. Cracked and damaged finishes: Changes in temperature can cause finishes to crack and peel, leaving your furniture vulnerable to further damage. Loose joints and structural instability: Over time, the joints in your furniture can loosen, compromising its stability and safety. Pest infestations: Certain wood-boring insects thrive in humid conditions, causing significant damage to wooden furniture.
Upholstery wear and tear: Daily use takes a toll on upholstery, leading to tears, stains, and overall deterioration.

These issues not only affect the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but can also lead to significant costs for replacements and pose safety risks.

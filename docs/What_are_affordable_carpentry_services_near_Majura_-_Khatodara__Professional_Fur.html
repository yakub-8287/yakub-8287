Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's hot and humid climate can significantly impact furniture. Common issues include:

Wood warping and cracking: Fluctuations in temperature and humidity can cause wood to expand and contract, leading to warping, cracks, and splits. Pest infestation: The humid environment can attract termites and other wood-boring insects, damaging your furniture. Loose joints and broken components: Years of use can loosen joints and break components, compromising the structural integrity of your furniture. Upholstery damage: Sun exposure and wear and tear can fade and damage upholstery fabrics.

These problems not only affect the aesthetics of your furniture but can also pose safety risks. Ignoring these issues can lead to more expensive repairs down the line.

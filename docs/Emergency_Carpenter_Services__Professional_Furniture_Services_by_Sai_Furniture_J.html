Common Furniture Issues in Surat: A Problem Statement

Surat, with its vibrant culture and diverse climate, presents unique challenges for furniture. The high humidity and fluctuating temperatures can lead to:

Wood warping and cracking: Especially prevalent in older, traditional Gujarati furniture. Pest infestations: Termites and other insects can cause significant damage. Joint failures: Due to age, wear and tear, or sudden impacts. Upholstery damage: Tears, stains, and fading from sun exposure are common.

These issues not only detract from the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but can also pose safety risks and significant repair costs if left unaddressed. Ignoring these problems can lead to even larger and more expensive repairs down the line.

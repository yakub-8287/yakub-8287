2. The Problem: Furniture Challenges in Surat's Climate

Surat's unique climate, with its high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, presents significant challenges for furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and warping: The humidity can cause wood to expand and contract, leading to cracks and warping. Pest infestation: Warm, humid conditions are ideal breeding grounds for termites and other wood-boring insects. Fabric deterioration: Heat and humidity can damage upholstery fabrics, causing fading and weakening. High replacement costs: Replacing damaged furniture can be expensive, impacting your budget significantly.

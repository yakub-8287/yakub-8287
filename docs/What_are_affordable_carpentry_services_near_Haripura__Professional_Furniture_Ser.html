The Problem: Furniture Issues in Surat

Surat's climate, with its high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, puts significant stress on furniture. Common problems include:

Wood rot and warping: The humid climate can cause wood to swell, crack, and even rot, especially in older furniture. Insect infestation: Termites and other insects are a common threat, damaging the structure of your furniture. Loose joints and broken parts: Years of use can lead to loose joints, broken legs, and other structural damage. Outdated styles: You may wish to update your furniture to better suit your home and personal style.

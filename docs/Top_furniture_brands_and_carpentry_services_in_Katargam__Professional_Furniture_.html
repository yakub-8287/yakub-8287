Common Furniture Problems in Surat's Climate

Surat's humid climate and fluctuating temperatures pose significant challenges for furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and warping: High humidity can cause wood to absorb excess moisture, leading to rot and warping, especially in older pieces. Pest infestation: Warm and humid conditions create an ideal breeding ground for termites and other wood-boring insects. Fabric damage: Moisture can cause fabric to mildew and fade, impacting the appearance and longevity of upholstered furniture. Structural damage: Extreme temperature fluctuations can cause furniture to crack or weaken over time.

These issues not only affect the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but can also lead to costly repairs and potential safety hazards. Neglecting these problems can result in significant financial losses and potential risks to your family.

Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's humid climate and fluctuating temperatures can severely impact your furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and warping: High humidity causes wood to absorb moisture, leading to rot, warping, and structural damage. Pest infestations: Warm, humid conditions can attract termites and other wood-boring insects. Fading and discoloration: Sun exposure and humidity can cause fabrics and wood finishes to fade or discolor. Loose joints and broken parts: Regular wear and tear, coupled with climate effects, weaken joints and cause breakage.

These problems not only detract from the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but can also be costly to repair if not addressed promptly.

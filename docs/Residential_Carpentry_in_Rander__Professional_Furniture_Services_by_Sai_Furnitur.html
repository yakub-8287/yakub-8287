The Challenges of Furniture in Surat's Climate

Surat's hot and humid climate presents unique challenges for furniture. The relentless sun and monsoon season can cause wood to warp, crack, and deteriorate prematurely. Common problems include:

Wood rot and insect infestation: Humidity can encourage the growth of mold and attract wood-boring insects. Cracked or warped surfaces: Extreme temperature fluctuations can cause wood to expand and contract, leading to cracks and warping. Faded finishes: Prolonged sun exposure can cause furniture finishes to fade and lose their luster.

These issues not only detract from the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but can also compromise its structural integrity, leading to costly repairs or replacements down the line. Ignoring these problems can also pose safety hazards, especially with unstable furniture.

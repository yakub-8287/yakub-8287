2. The Challenges of Furniture in Surat's Climate

Surat's unique climate, marked by high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, poses significant challenges to furniture. Common problems include:

Wood rot and warping: The humid climate can lead to wood damage, compromising the structural integrity of your pieces. Pest infestations: Warm temperatures create ideal conditions for termites and other pests that can destroy furniture. Fabric deterioration: High humidity can cause fabric to mildew and fade, reducing the lifespan and aesthetic appeal. Mechanical failures: Fluctuations in temperature can affect the functionality of furniture mechanisms.

These issues can lead to expensive repairs or even the need for complete replacements, creating significant financial and safety concerns.

Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's unique climate, characterized by high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, poses significant challenges to furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and warping: The humidity can cause wood to absorb moisture, leading to rot, warping, and structural damage. Pest infestations: Termites and other wood-boring insects thrive in humid conditions, causing significant damage to furniture. Fabric fading and deterioration: The strong sunlight can fade upholstery and cause fabrics to weaken over time. Loose joints and structural damage: General wear and tear can cause furniture to become unstable and unsafe.

These problems not only affect the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but can also lead to significant repair costs and safety concerns. Ignoring these issues can result in costly replacements down the line.

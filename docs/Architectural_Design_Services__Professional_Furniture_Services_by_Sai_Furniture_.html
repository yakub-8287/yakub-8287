Common Furniture Problems in Surat: The Impact of Climate and Time

Surat's climate, characterized by high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, presents unique challenges for furniture. Wood can warp, crack, or become infested with pests. Upholstery can fade, mildew, or wear down prematurely. These problems not only impact the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but can also lead to safety hazards and costly repairs down the line. For example, a damaged chair leg could lead to a fall, while termite damage can compromise the structural integrity of an entire piece.

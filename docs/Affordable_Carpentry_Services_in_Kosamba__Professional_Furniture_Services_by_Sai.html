Common Furniture Problems in Surat & Kosamba

The humid climate of Surat and Kosamba can significantly impact the lifespan of your furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and decay: Excessive moisture weakens wood, leading to cracks, warping, and structural damage. Pest infestations: Termites and other wood-boring insects are prevalent in the region, causing significant damage. Sun damage: Prolonged exposure to sunlight can fade finishes and weaken wood fibers. General wear and tear: Everyday use inevitably leads to scratches, dents, and loose joints.

These issues not only affect the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but can also pose safety risks and lead to costly replacements.

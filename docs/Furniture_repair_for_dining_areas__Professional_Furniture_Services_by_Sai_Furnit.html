Common Furniture Issues in Surat

Surat's climate, characterized by high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, can significantly impact your furniture. Common problems include:

Wood rot and warping: Excess moisture can cause wood to swell, crack, or even rot, especially in older pieces. Faded finishes: Prolonged sun exposure leads to fading and discoloration, diminishing the beauty of your furniture. Loose joints and wobbly legs: Over time, the glue in joints weakens, causing instability and safety hazards. Scratches and dents: Daily wear and tear inevitably leads to surface damage.

These issues can not only affect the aesthetics of your dining set but also compromise its structural integrity, potentially leading to costly replacements. Ignoring these problems can also pose safety risks, particularly for children and the elderly.

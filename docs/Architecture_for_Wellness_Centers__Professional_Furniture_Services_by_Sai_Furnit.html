2. Problem Statement: Furniture Challenges in Surat's Climate

Surat's humid climate presents unique challenges for furniture. The heat and moisture can lead to:

Wood rot and warping: Particularly affecting traditional Gujarati furniture styles. Mildew and mold growth: Compromising hygiene and potentially impacting client health. Fabric damage: Upholstery can become stained, faded, and lose its structural integrity. Pest infestation: Wood-boring insects thrive in humid conditions.

These issues not only affect the aesthetic appeal of your wellness center but also pose safety risks and incur significant replacement or repair costs.

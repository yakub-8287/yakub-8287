Common Furniture Issues in Surat: The Challenge of Preservation

Surat's humid climate and fluctuating temperatures present significant challenges to preserving antique furniture. Common problems include:

Wood rot and decay: Excess moisture leads to warping, cracking, and the infestation of wood-boring insects. Pest infestations: Termites and other pests thrive in warm, humid conditions, damaging furniture structures. Fabric deterioration: Humidity weakens fabrics, causing discoloration, mold, and structural damage. UV damage: Sunlight can fade finishes and weaken wood fibers over time.

Ignoring these issues can lead to significant financial loss, potential safety hazards, and the irreplaceable loss of valuable heritage pieces. Furthermore, the cost of repairs often increases exponentially with neglect.

The Problem with Damaged Furniture in Surat

Surat's climate, characterized by high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, can take a toll on furniture. Common problems include:

Wood rot and warping: The humid summers can cause wood to swell and warp, compromising structural integrity. Insect infestation: Certain insects thrive in humid conditions, damaging furniture with holes and tunnels. Cracked and chipped finishes: Temperature fluctuations cause expansion and contraction, leading to cracks and chips in the finish. Loose joints and broken components: Years of use inevitably lead to wear and tear, resulting in loose joints and broken parts.

Ignoring these issues can lead to further damage, escalating repair costs and potentially posing safety hazards.

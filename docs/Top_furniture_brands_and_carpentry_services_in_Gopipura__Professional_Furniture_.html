2. Common Furniture Problems in Surat: Understanding the Challenges

Surat's climate, with its fluctuating humidity and temperatures, can significantly impact furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and warping: Excessive moisture can cause wood to rot, crack, and warp, especially in older pieces. Pest infestations: Warm, humid conditions create a breeding ground for termites and other wood-boring insects, leading to structural damage. Fabric fading and wear: Intense sunlight and humidity can fade fabric and cause premature wear and tear on upholstery. Joint failure: Changes in temperature and humidity can loosen joints, making furniture unstable and unsafe.

These problems not only affect the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but also pose safety risks and incur significant repair costs.

Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's unique climate, with its fluctuating humidity and temperatures, presents significant challenges for furniture. Exposure to the monsoons can cause warping and rotting in wooden furniture, while intense summer heat can lead to cracking and fading. Dust and grime accumulated over time can also affect the appearance and longevity of your furniture. These issues not only impact aesthetics but can also lead to structural damage and safety concerns, potentially requiring costly repairs or replacements. Ignoring these problems can also compromise the structural integrity of your furniture, posing potential safety hazards, especially for antique pieces.

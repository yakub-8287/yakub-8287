2. Common Furniture Problems in Surat: Understanding the Challenges

Surat's warm and humid climate presents unique challenges for furniture. Common problems include:

Wood warping and cracking: Fluctuations in temperature and humidity can cause wood to expand and contract, leading to cracks and warping. Mildew and fungal growth: Excessive humidity can foster the growth of mildew and fungi, damaging the fabric and wooden structures of your furniture. Loose joints and broken components: Normal wear and tear, especially with heavy use, can loosen joints and lead to broken parts. Damaged upholstery: Spills, stains, and general wear and tear affect the look and longevity of your furniture's upholstery. Insect infestations: Certain insects can infest wooden furniture, causing significant damage if left unchecked.

These issues can not only impact the aesthetics of your furniture but also pose safety concerns, potentially leading to costly repairs or replacements if neglected.

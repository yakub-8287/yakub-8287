Common Furniture Problems in Surat: The Challenges of Gujarat's Climate

Surat's humid climate and fluctuating temperatures pose significant challenges to wooden furniture. Excessive moisture can lead to warping, cracking, and the growth of mold and mildew. Temperature variations cause wood to expand and contract, resulting in splits and structural damage. These issues not only diminish the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but also compromise its structural integrity, potentially leading to costly repairs or even complete replacement. Ignoring these problems can also pose safety risks, especially with damaged chairs or tables.

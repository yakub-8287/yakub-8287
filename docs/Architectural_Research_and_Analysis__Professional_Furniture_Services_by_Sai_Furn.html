Common Furniture Problems in Surat: A Problem Statement

Surat's climate, characterized by high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, presents unique challenges for furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and decay: The humid climate encourages the growth of mold and fungi, weakening wooden structures. Insect infestation: Termites and other wood-boring insects thrive in warm, damp environments, compromising the integrity of furniture. Structural damage: Over time, aging and improper care can lead to cracks, warping, and loose joints, especially in older pieces. Fabric deterioration: High humidity and intense sunlight can cause upholstery fabrics to fade, weaken, and tear. Aesthetic degradation: Scratches, stains, and general wear and tear diminish the beauty and value of your furniture.

Ignoring these problems can lead to costly repairs or even irreversible damage, not to mention safety hazards.

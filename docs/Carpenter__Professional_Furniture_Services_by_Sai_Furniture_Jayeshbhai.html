Common Furniture Problems in Surat's Climate

Surat's hot and humid climate presents unique challenges to furniture. Common problems include:

Wood warping and cracking: Fluctuating temperatures and humidity levels can cause wooden furniture to warp, crack, and lose its structural integrity. Pest infestations: The warm, humid climate can attract termites and other wood-boring insects, damaging your valuable pieces. Fading and discoloration: Sunlight and humidity can cause furniture to fade and discolor over time, diminishing its aesthetic appeal. Loose joints and broken components: Daily wear and tear, coupled with the effects of the climate, can lead to furniture instability and potential safety hazards.

These issues not only affect the appearance of your furniture but also lead to costly repairs or premature replacements. Ignoring these problems can result in significant expenses down the line.

The Problem with Unsustainable Furniture Practices in Surat

Surat, with its vibrant climate and unique architectural styles, presents specific challenges to furniture longevity. The high humidity and fluctuating temperatures can cause significant damage to furniture if not properly maintained. Common issues include wood rot, insect infestation, and structural weakening. Replacing damaged furniture not only incurs high costs but also contributes to environmental waste. Ignoring these problems can also compromise safety, leading to potential accidents from unstable pieces. Many traditional furniture repair methods may not address these issues effectively, impacting both the longevity of your furniture and the overall aesthetics of your home or business.

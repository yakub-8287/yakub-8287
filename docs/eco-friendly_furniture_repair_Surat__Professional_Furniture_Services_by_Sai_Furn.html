2. The Challenges of Furniture in Surat's Climate

Surat, with its humid climate and fluctuating temperatures, presents unique challenges for furniture. Common problems include:

Cracked and faded finishes: Extreme temperatures and sun exposure can cause finishes to crack, peel, and fade, compromising the aesthetics and structural integrity of your furniture. Pest infestations: Certain climatic conditions can attract wood-boring insects and other pests that damage furniture. High repair costs: Replacing damaged furniture can be expensive, especially when dealing with antique or unique pieces.

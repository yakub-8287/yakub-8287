Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's hot and humid climate poses significant challenges to furniture. Wood can warp, fabrics can mildew, and even sturdy pieces can degrade over time. Common issues include:

Wood rot and warping: High humidity weakens wood, leading to rot and warping, especially in older pieces. Fabric damage: Mildew and moisture stains are prevalent in humid conditions, affecting upholstery and other fabrics. Pest infestation: The climate can be conducive to pest infestations, damaging furniture structure. Loss of functionality: Damaged or outdated furniture can become unsafe and inefficient.

Ignoring these issues can lead to costly repairs, safety concerns, and the need for premature replacements.

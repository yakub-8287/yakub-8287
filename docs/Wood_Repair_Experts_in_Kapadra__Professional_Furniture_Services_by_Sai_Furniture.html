2. Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's humid climate and fluctuating temperatures can significantly impact the condition of your furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and decay: Excess moisture weakens wood fibers, leading to rot and structural damage. Cracks and splits: Changes in temperature and humidity can cause wood to expand and contract, resulting in cracks. Scratches and dents: Everyday wear and tear can leave your furniture looking less than perfect. Loose joints and instability: Over time, the joints in your furniture can weaken, leading to instability and potential collapse. Pest infestation: Certain wood-boring insects thrive in humid conditions and can cause extensive damage.

These problems not only detract from the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but can also pose safety hazards and lead to costly replacements.

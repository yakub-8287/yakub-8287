Common Problems with Garden Furniture in Surat

Surat's hot and humid climate, coupled with occasional monsoons, takes its toll on outdoor furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and decay: High humidity promotes fungal growth, leading to weakening and structural damage. Wicker damage: Sun exposure and rain can cause wicker to become brittle and break. Metal rust and corrosion: Exposure to moisture accelerates rust formation on metal frames. Fabric fading and tearing: Sunlight and constant exposure to the elements wear down fabric cushions. Loose joints and structural instability: Over time, constant use and weather conditions loosen joints and compromise furniture stability, creating safety hazards.

These problems not only affect the aesthetics of your garden but also pose safety risks. Ignoring damage can lead to costly replacements down the line.

Common Furniture Problems in Surat's Climate

Surat's humid climate and fluctuating temperatures can significantly impact the longevity and condition of your furniture. Common problems include:

Wood warping and cracking: The high humidity can cause wood to expand and contract, leading to cracks and warping. Pest infestation: Warm, humid conditions create ideal breeding grounds for termites and other wood-boring insects. Weakened joints: Changes in temperature and humidity can weaken the joints in furniture, causing instability and damage. Fading and discoloration: Exposure to sunlight and humidity can cause furniture finishes to fade and discolor.

These issues not only affect the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but also pose safety risks and can lead to costly replacements.

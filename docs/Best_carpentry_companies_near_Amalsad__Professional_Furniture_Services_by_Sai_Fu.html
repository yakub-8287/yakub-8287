Common Furniture Problems in Surat: Addressing the Challenges

Surat's climate, characterized by high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, takes a toll on furniture. Common issues include:

Wood warping and cracking: Humidity can cause wood to expand and contract, leading to unsightly warping and cracks. Pest infestations: The warm, humid climate can attract wood-boring insects, causing structural damage. Fading and discoloration: Sun exposure and dust can fade the finish of your furniture, diminishing its aesthetic appeal. Loose joints and broken components: Regular wear and tear can weaken joints and cause components to break.

These problems not only affect the appearance of your furniture but also pose safety risks and can lead to costly replacements.

2. Problem Statement: The Challenges of Furniture in Surat's Climate

Surat, with its warm climate and fluctuating humidity, presents unique challenges for furniture. Common issues include:

Wood warping and cracking: The intense heat and humidity can cause wood to expand and contract, leading to damage and decreased lifespan. Fabric deterioration: High temperatures and sunlight can fade and damage upholstery fabrics, requiring frequent replacements. Pest infestations: Certain insects thrive in warm, humid environments and can damage furniture if not properly protected. High replacement costs: Replacing damaged furniture can be costly, especially for bespoke or antique pieces.

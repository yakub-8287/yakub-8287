Common Furniture Problems in Surat: The Impact of Climate and Time

Surat's hot and humid climate presents unique challenges to wooden furniture. Common problems include:

Wood warping and cracking: Fluctuations in temperature and humidity cause wood to expand and contract, leading to cracks and warping. Pest infestation: Warm, humid conditions can attract termites and other wood-boring insects, damaging your furniture. Fading and discoloration: Sunlight and exposure to the elements can cause furniture to fade and lose its vibrancy. Loose joints and broken parts: Normal wear and tear, especially with older pieces, can lead to structural instability.

These problems can lead to significant costs, from expensive repairs to complete furniture replacement. Ignoring these issues can also create safety hazards, such as unstable chairs or broken tables.

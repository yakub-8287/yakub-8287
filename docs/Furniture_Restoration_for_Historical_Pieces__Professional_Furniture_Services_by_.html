Common Furniture Issues in Surat & Their Impact

Surat's climate, characterized by high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, poses significant challenges to antique furniture. Common problems include:

Wood rot and decay: Humidity leads to mold growth and structural damage. Cracked and warped surfaces: Temperature fluctuations cause expansion and contraction. Loose joints and broken components: Years of use take their toll. Faded finishes and worn upholstery: Exposure to sunlight and dust diminishes aesthetic appeal.

Ignoring these issues can lead to further damage, increasing restoration costs and potentially rendering the piece irreparable. Furthermore, damaged furniture can present safety hazards, such as splinters or unstable structures.

The Challenges of Furniture in Surat's Climate

Surat, with its warm climate and humidity, presents unique challenges for furniture. Common issues include:

Wood warping and cracking: Fluctuating temperatures and humidity can cause wood to expand and contract, leading to damage. Pest infestations: Warm, humid conditions create ideal breeding grounds for wood-boring insects. Fabric fading and deterioration: Sunlight and humidity can fade fabrics and weaken their structure. High replacement costs: Replacing damaged furniture can be expensive, especially if you are looking for high-quality pieces.

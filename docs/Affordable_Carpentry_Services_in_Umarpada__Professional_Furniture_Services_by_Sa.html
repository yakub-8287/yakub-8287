Common Furniture Problems in Surat

The hot and humid climate of Surat, Gujarat, presents unique challenges for furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and warping: High humidity can cause wood to expand and contract, leading to cracking, warping, and rot, especially in older pieces. Pest infestation: Termites and other insects are a significant threat to wooden furniture in the region. Loose joints and broken components: Everyday wear and tear can weaken joints and damage components, making furniture unstable and unsafe. Outdated styles: Many homes in Surat feature traditional furniture styles that may need updating to reflect current design trends.

These issues not only affect the aesthetics of your furniture but also pose safety concerns and can lead to costly replacements.

The Problem: Surat's Furniture Challenges and Environmental Concerns

Surat, with its hot and humid climate, presents unique challenges for furniture. High humidity can lead to warping, swelling, and pest infestations. Extreme temperatures can cause cracking and fading, especially in traditional wooden furniture common in Gujarat. Replacing damaged furniture not only costs significantly but also contributes to landfill waste. Moreover, using low-quality materials and improper disposal methods pose safety risks to the environment and your family.

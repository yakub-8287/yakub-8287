Common Furniture Problems in Surat

The hot and humid climate of Surat presents unique challenges for furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and warping: High humidity can cause wood to expand and crack, leading to structural damage. Pest infestations: Termites and other insects thrive in humid conditions, damaging furniture frames and finishes. Sun damage: Exposure to direct sunlight can fade finishes and weaken wood. General wear and tear: Everyday use can cause scratches, dents, and loose joints.

These problems not only affect the aesthetics of your furniture but can also pose safety risks and lead to costly replacements. Ignoring minor issues can often lead to more extensive and expensive repairs down the line.

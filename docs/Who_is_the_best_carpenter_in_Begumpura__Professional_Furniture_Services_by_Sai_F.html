The Problem: Furniture Issues in Surat's Humid Climate

Surat's climate, characterized by high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, presents unique challenges for furniture. Common problems include:

Wood rot and warping: The dampness can cause wood to expand, leading to warping, cracking, and eventually rot, especially in older pieces. Pest infestation: High humidity provides ideal breeding grounds for termites and other wood-boring insects. Fading and discoloration: Sunlight and humidity can cause furniture finishes to fade and lose their vibrancy. Loose joints and broken parts: Years of wear and tear, exacerbated by climate changes, can result in structural instability.

These issues not only affect the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but can also lead to significant repair costs and safety hazards.

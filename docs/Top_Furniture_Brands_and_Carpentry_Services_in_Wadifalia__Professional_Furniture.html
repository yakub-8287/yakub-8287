Common Furniture Problems in Surat & Wadifalia

Surat, with its humid climate and fluctuating temperatures, presents unique challenges for furniture. Common problems include:

Wood rot and warping: The humidity can cause wood to swell, crack, or rot, especially in older pieces. Insect infestation: Certain insects thrive in humid conditions and can damage wooden furniture. Upholstery damage: Exposure to sunlight and humidity can fade and deteriorate fabric. Structural damage: Wear and tear, accidents, or improper handling can compromise the structural integrity of furniture. These problems can lead to significant costs for repairs or replacements, as well as safety hazards.

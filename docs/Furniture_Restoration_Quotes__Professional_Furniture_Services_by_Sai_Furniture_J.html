Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's hot and humid climate presents unique challenges for furniture. Monsoons and temperature fluctuations can cause:

Wood rot and warping: High humidity encourages fungal growth and warping, especially in older, untreated wood. Insect infestation: Warm, damp conditions attract pests like termites and wood-boring beetles. Fading and discoloration: Prolonged sun exposure can fade the finish and color of your furniture. Upholstery damage: Moisture can lead to mold and mildew growth in upholstery, impacting its aesthetic appeal and structural integrity.

Ignoring these issues can lead to costly repairs or even the need for furniture replacement. Early intervention through professional furniture restoration is crucial for preserving your valued pieces and avoiding further damage.

The Problem: Damaged Furniture in Surat's Climate

Surat's climate presents unique challenges for furniture. The high humidity and fluctuating temperatures can lead to several common issues:

Wood rot and warping: Excessive moisture causes wood to swell, crack, and rot, especially in older pieces. Fabric fading and staining: Sun exposure and humidity can damage upholstery fabrics, leaving them faded, stained, or torn. Pest infestation: Warm, humid conditions can attract termites and other wood-boring insects, leading to significant damage. Joint failure: Changes in temperature and humidity can weaken the joints in furniture, causing them to become loose or break.

These problems not only diminish the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but can also pose safety concerns and lead to costly replacements.

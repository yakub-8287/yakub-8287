Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's climate, with its fluctuating humidity and temperatures, can significantly impact the condition of wooden furniture. From minor scratches and dents to severe water damage and termite infestations, many furniture pieces suffer over time. The intense summer heat can cause wood to dry out and crack, while the monsoon season brings the risk of warping and mold. These issues not only detract from the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but can also lead to costly repairs or even replacement if left untreated. Ignoring these problems can also pose safety hazards, like unstable chairs or broken table legs.

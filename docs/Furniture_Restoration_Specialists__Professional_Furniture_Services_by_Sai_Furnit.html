2. Common Furniture Problems in Surat: Understanding the Challenges

Surat's unique climate, characterized by high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, poses significant challenges to furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and damage: Excess moisture can lead to warping, cracking, and the growth of mold, particularly in older wooden furniture. Upholstery wear and tear: Dust, humidity, and daily use contribute to fading, staining, and damage to upholstery fabrics. Insect infestation: Warm and humid conditions can attract wood-boring insects, causing structural damage and compromising the integrity of your furniture. Structural damage: Accidents or age can cause damage to joints, legs, or other structural components.

These issues not only affect the aesthetics of your furniture but can also lead to costly repairs if left untreated. Neglecting these problems can also create safety hazards.

Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's climate, with its fluctuating humidity and temperatures, can wreak havoc on wooden furniture. Common problems include:

Wood rot and decay: High humidity can lead to fungal growth and structural damage. Cracks and splits: Sudden temperature changes cause wood to expand and contract, resulting in cracks. Scratches and water damage: Everyday accidents can leave lasting marks. Loose joints and instability: Years of use can weaken joints, compromising stability and safety.

These issues not only detract from the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but can also pose safety risks and lead to costly replacements.

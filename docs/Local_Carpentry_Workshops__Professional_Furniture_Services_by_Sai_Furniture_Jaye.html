Common Furniture Problems in Surat: The Need for Expert Repair

The hot and humid climate of Surat, Gujarat can take a toll on your furniture. Common problems include:

Wood rot and warping: High humidity leads to moisture damage, causing wood to swell, crack, and rot. Pest infestations: Termites and other insects thrive in warm, humid conditions, damaging furniture structures. Sun damage: Prolonged sun exposure can fade finishes and crack wood surfaces. General wear and tear: Daily use inevitably leads to scratches, dents, and loose joints.

Ignoring these issues can lead to costly replacements down the line, not to mention safety hazards. A professional assessment and timely repair are essential for preserving your furniture's value and longevity.

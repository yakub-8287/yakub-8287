2. The Problem: Surat's Climate and its Impact on Your Furniture

Surat's hot and humid climate, coupled with periods of intense sunlight and occasional monsoon rains, takes a toll on outdoor furniture. Wicker can become brittle, wood can rot, and metal can rust. This damage not only diminishes the aesthetic appeal of your patio or garden but also poses safety risks. Ignoring these issues leads to costly replacements down the line. Imagine the cost of replacing a beautiful teakwood dining set – a significant investment lost to neglect.

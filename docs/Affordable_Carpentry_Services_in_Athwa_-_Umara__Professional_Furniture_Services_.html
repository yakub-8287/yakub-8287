Common Furniture Problems in Surat: The Challenges of Our Climate

Surat's humid climate and fluctuating temperatures can significantly impact the lifespan of your furniture. Common problems include:

Wood rot and warping: Excess moisture can cause wood to swell, crack, and even rot, especially in older pieces. Pest infestations: Warm, humid conditions can attract termites and other wood-boring insects, damaging your furniture. Loose joints and broken components: Normal wear and tear, coupled with environmental stress, can lead to structural issues. Faded finishes and peeling paint: Sun exposure and humidity can dull the finish and cause paint to peel, reducing the aesthetic appeal of your furniture. Damaged upholstery: Spills, tears, and general wear and tear can negatively impact your furniture.

These issues not only compromise the functionality and aesthetic value of your furniture but can also pose safety hazards. Ignoring these problems can lead to more expensive repairs down the line.

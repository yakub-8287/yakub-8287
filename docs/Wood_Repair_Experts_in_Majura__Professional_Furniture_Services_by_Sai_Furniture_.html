2. Common Furniture Problems in Surat: Understanding the Challenges

The humid climate and fluctuating temperatures in Surat can take a toll on wooden furniture. Common problems include:

Cracked or warped wood: Extreme humidity can cause wood to expand and contract, leading to cracks and warping. Scratches and dents: Daily wear and tear can leave your furniture looking less than perfect. Water damage: Spills and exposure to moisture can cause significant damage, leading to rot and discoloration. Loose joints and broken components: Over time, the glue in furniture joints can weaken, causing instability and breakage. Pest infestation: Termites and other pests can cause extensive damage to wooden furniture, often going unnoticed until significant damage has been done.

These issues not only affect the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but can also compromise its structural integrity and safety, leading to costly replacements.

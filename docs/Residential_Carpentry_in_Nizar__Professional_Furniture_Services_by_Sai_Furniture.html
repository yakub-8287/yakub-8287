Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's humid climate and fluctuating temperatures pose unique challenges to furniture. Common problems include:

Wood rot and warping: High humidity can lead to wood absorbing moisture, causing warping, cracking, and rot, especially in older pieces. Pest infestations: Warm, humid conditions can attract termites and other wood-boring insects, damaging your valuable furniture. Sun damage: Prolonged exposure to direct sunlight can fade and crack finishes. Loose joints and broken parts: Everyday wear and tear can cause structural damage.

These issues can not only diminish the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but also lead to significant costs for replacements or extensive repairs. Ignoring these problems can also compromise safety, especially with damaged chairs or unstable structures.

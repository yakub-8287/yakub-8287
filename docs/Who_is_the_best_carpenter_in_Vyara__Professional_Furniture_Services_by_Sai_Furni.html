The Problem: Furniture Issues in Surat's Climate

Surat's unique climate, with its high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, presents significant challenges to furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and warping: High humidity can cause wood to absorb moisture, leading to rot and warping, particularly in older furniture pieces. Pest infestations: Warm temperatures create ideal breeding grounds for wood-boring insects, damaging furniture beyond repair if left untreated. Cracks and splits: Temperature fluctuations cause wood to expand and contract, leading to cracks and splits in the furniture's structure. Loose joints and broken parts: Normal wear and tear, coupled with climate challenges, can weaken joints and cause parts to break.

These issues not only impact the aesthetics of your furniture but also pose safety risks and significant repair costs.

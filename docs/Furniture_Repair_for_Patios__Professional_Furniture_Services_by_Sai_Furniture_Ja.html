2. Common Patio Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's hot, humid climate, combined with exposure to dust and sunlight, can significantly damage patio furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and decay: Humidity can lead to fungal growth and wood deterioration. Wicker and rattan damage: Weakened fibers and broken strands are common. Metal rust and corrosion: Exposure to moisture leads to rust formation. Fabric fading and tearing: Sunlight and prolonged use can damage cushions and upholstery. Loose joints and broken structures: Years of use and harsh weather can lead to instability.

Ignoring these issues can lead to increased repair costs later, compromising safety and the aesthetic appeal of your patio.

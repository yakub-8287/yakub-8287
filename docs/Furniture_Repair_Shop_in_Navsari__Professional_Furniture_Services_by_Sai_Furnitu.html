2. Common Furniture Problems in Surat and Their Solutions

Surat's climate, with its high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, can take a toll on your furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and damage: Excessive moisture can lead to warping, cracking, and even rot in wooden furniture. Upholstery wear and tear: Sunlight and regular use contribute to fading, staining, and fabric damage. Loose joints and broken components: Over time, furniture can become unstable due to wear and tear. Pest infestation: Termites and other pests can cause significant damage if left untreated.

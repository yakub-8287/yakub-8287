Common Furniture Problems in Surat & The Solutions Offered

The humid Surat climate can wreak havoc on your furniture. From warping wood to loose joints and pest infestations, many homes face these challenges. The high temperatures and humidity can lead to:

Pest infestations: Termites and other insects can damage furniture structures. Upholstery issues: Moisture can cause mildew and mold growth in fabric upholstery.

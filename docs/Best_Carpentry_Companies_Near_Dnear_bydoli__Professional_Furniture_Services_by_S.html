Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's hot and humid climate can wreak havoc on furniture. Common issues include:

Pest infestations: Termites and other insects thrive in humid conditions, causing significant damage. Upholstery issues: Heat and humidity can cause fabrics to fade, tear, or become musty. Loose joints and structural damage: Years of use and temperature fluctuations can loosen joints and compromise structural integrity.

These problems not only detract from the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but also incur significant repair costs and potential safety hazards.

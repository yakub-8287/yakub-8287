The Furniture Problems Faced by Surat Residents

Surat's climate, with its fluctuating humidity and temperatures, takes a toll on furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and warping: The intense humidity can cause wood to expand and contract, leading to cracking, warping, and even rot. Pest infestation: Termites and other wood-boring insects are prevalent in the region, causing significant damage. Upholstery wear and tear: Frequent use and exposure to sunlight can fade and damage upholstery. Loose joints and broken pieces: Normal wear and tear can cause furniture to become unstable or break.

These issues not only impact the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but also pose safety hazards and can lead to costly replacements.

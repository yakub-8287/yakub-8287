Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's humid climate and fluctuating temperatures can take a toll on your cherished furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and warping: High humidity can cause wood to absorb moisture, leading to rot and warping. Insect infestation: Warm temperatures can attract insects that damage wooden furniture. Sun damage: Intense sunlight can fade finishes and damage the wood. General wear and tear: Everyday use can cause scratches, dents, and loose joints.

These issues not only affect the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but can also compromise its structural integrity, leading to costly replacements. Ignoring these problems can even lead to safety hazards.

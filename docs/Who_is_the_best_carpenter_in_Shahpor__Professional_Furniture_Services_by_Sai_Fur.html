Common Furniture Problems in Surat and Their Solutions

Surat, with its vibrant climate, presents unique challenges for furniture. The humidity and fluctuating temperatures can lead to various issues:

Insect infestation: Certain insects thrive in humid conditions, damaging wooden furniture. Worn-out finishes: Regular use and exposure to sunlight can cause furniture finishes to fade or chip. Broken joints and hardware: Daily use can put stress on joints and hardware, eventually leading to breakage.

Common Furniture Problems in Surat & Mahidharpura

Surat, with its humid climate and fluctuating temperatures, presents unique challenges for furniture. This is particularly true in older homes and traditional Gujarati style furniture. These issues not only impact the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but can also lead to safety hazards, such as loose joints or splintering wood.

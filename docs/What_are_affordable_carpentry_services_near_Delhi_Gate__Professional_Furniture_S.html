Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's climate, characterized by high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, can significantly affect furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and warping: The dampness can cause wood to rot and lose its structural integrity, leading to cracks and instability. Pest infestation: Termites and other wood-boring insects thrive in humid conditions, damaging furniture from within. Paint peeling and fading: The sun and humidity can cause paint to peel and fade, leaving your furniture looking old and worn. Loose joints and broken parts: Normal wear and tear, coupled with climate challenges, often leads to loose joints and broken components.

These issues can quickly become costly if not addressed promptly, leading to the need for complete furniture replacements. Furthermore, attempting DIY repairs can often lead to further damage or safety hazards.

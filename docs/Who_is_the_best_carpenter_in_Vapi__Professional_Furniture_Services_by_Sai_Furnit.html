Common Furniture Problems in Surat and the Surrounding Areas

The humid climate of Surat, and indeed much of Gujarat, presents unique challenges for furniture. Exposure to fluctuating temperatures and high humidity can lead to:

Wood rot and warping: This is particularly common in older pieces or those made from less durable wood types. Insect infestation: Termites and other wood-boring insects are a significant threat to furniture in the region. Cracks and splits: Changes in temperature and humidity can cause wood to expand and contract, resulting in cracks and splits. Loose joints and broken parts: Daily wear and tear can weaken furniture joints and lead to breakage.

These problems not only affect the appearance of your furniture but also compromise its structural integrity and safety. Ignoring these issues can lead to increased costs in the long run. Repairing or restoring your furniture promptly is essential.

Common Furniture Challenges in Surat's Climate

Surat's unique climate presents specific challenges for furniture. High humidity and fluctuating temperatures can lead to:

Wood rot and warping: The humid Surat summers can severely damage wooden furniture, causing warping, cracking, and even rot if not properly maintained. Pest infestation: Warm and humid conditions create ideal breeding grounds for insects and pests that can damage furniture and compromise its structural integrity. Fabric deterioration: High humidity can lead to mold and mildew growth on fabrics, affecting both the appearance and lifespan of upholstered furniture. High Repair Costs: Neglecting these issues can lead to expensive repairs or even complete furniture replacement down the line.

The Challenges Facing Surat's Furniture

Surat's unique climate presents specific challenges for furniture. The high humidity and fluctuating temperatures can cause warping, cracking, and deterioration in wooden furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and infestation: The damp climate encourages wood-boring insects and fungal growth. Fabric damage: Mold and mildew can easily develop on upholstery, impacting both appearance and hygiene. Structural weakness: Exposure to the elements can weaken joints and frames.

Ignoring these problems leads to costly replacements and compromises the overall aesthetic of your home, which could be in line with the latest future trends in architecture.

Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's humid climate and fluctuating temperatures present unique challenges for furniture. Common problems include:

Wood rot and warping: High humidity can cause wood to absorb moisture, leading to rot and warping, especially in older pieces. Pest infestations: Termites and other wood-boring insects thrive in warm, humid environments, damaging furniture structures. Cracked and broken joints: Years of use, along with seasonal climate changes, often lead to cracked or broken joints and damaged finishes. Faded or damaged finishes: The Surat sun can cause fading and deterioration of furniture finishes over time.

These issues not only affect the aesthetics of your furniture but can also compromise its structural integrity, leading to safety concerns and costly replacements.

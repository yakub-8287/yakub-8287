Common Furniture Problems in Surat: The Challenge

Surat's unique climate, characterized by high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, presents significant challenges to furniture. Common problems include:

Wood rot and warping: Excessive moisture can cause wood to expand, crack, and rot, especially in traditional Gujarati furniture pieces. Pest infestations: Warm, humid conditions can attract termites and other pests, damaging furniture structures. Fabric fading and deterioration: Sunlight and humidity can quickly fade and damage upholstery fabrics. Metal rust and corrosion: The damp climate accelerates the deterioration of metal furniture components.

Ignoring these issues can lead to costly repairs or even complete furniture replacement, impacting both your budget and the aesthetic integrity of your home.

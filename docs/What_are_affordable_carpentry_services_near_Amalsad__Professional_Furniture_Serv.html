Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's climate, characterized by high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, can significantly impact the condition of your furniture. Common problems include:

Wood rot and warping: Excess moisture weakens wood, leading to rot and distortion. Pest infestations: Warm, humid conditions attract termites and other wood-boring insects. Cracked and damaged joints: Changes in temperature and humidity can cause furniture joints to loosen or break. Faded finishes: Sunlight and humidity can lead to discoloration and damage to furniture finishes.

These issues not only affect the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but can also compromise its structural integrity and safety, leading to costly repairs or replacements down the line.

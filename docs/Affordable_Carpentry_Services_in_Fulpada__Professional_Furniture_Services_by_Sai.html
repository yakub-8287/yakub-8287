Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's hot and humid climate can significantly impact the longevity of your furniture. Common problems include:

Wood rot and warping: High humidity causes wood to absorb moisture, leading to rotting and warping, especially in older pieces. Pest infestation: Warm temperatures create a breeding ground for termites and other wood-boring insects. Loose joints and broken pieces: Daily wear and tear, coupled with environmental factors, can weaken furniture structures. Damaged finishes: Sun exposure and humidity can cause paint or varnish to peel or crack.

These issues can lead to costly repairs or even necessitate replacing your cherished furniture. Addressing these problems promptly is crucial to prevent further damage and maintain your home’s aesthetic appeal.

Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's climate, characterized by high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, presents unique challenges for furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and warping: The humid climate can cause wood to absorb moisture, leading to rot and warping, especially in older furniture pieces. Pest infestation: Termites and other pests thrive in humid conditions, damaging wooden structures. Cracked and faded finishes: Exposure to sunlight and temperature fluctuations can cause paint and varnish to crack and fade, impacting the aesthetics and longevity of your furniture. Loose joints and structural damage: Over time, furniture can suffer from wear and tear, resulting in loose joints and structural instability.

2. Problem Statement: Common Garden Furniture Issues in Surat

The Surat climate, with its humidity and fluctuating temperatures, can significantly impact the longevity of your garden furniture. Exposure to the sun and rain can cause:

Wood rot and decay: Moisture penetrates the wood, leading to cracks, splintering, and structural weakness. Fading and peeling paint: Constant exposure to UV rays causes paint to lose its vibrancy and peel, leaving your furniture looking dull and unattractive.

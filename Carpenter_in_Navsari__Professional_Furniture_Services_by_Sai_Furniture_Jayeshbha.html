Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's climate, with its fluctuating humidity and temperatures, can take a toll on your furniture. Common problems include:

Wood rot and warping: High humidity can cause wood to absorb moisture, leading to rot and warping, particularly in older furniture. Cracked and damaged surfaces: Temperature fluctuations can cause wood to expand and contract, resulting in cracks and damage. Pest infestation: Certain insects thrive in humid conditions, damaging wooden furniture. Loose joints and broken components: Years of use and wear can cause furniture joints to loosen and components to break.

These issues not only affect the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but also pose safety concerns, and often result in costly replacements.

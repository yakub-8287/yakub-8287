2. The Challenges Faced by Furniture in Surat

Surat, with its vibrant culture and unique climate, presents certain challenges for furniture. The high humidity and fluctuating temperatures can lead to various issues, including:

Wood rot and warping: The humid climate can cause wood to absorb moisture, leading to warping, cracking, and ultimately, rotting. Pest infestations: High humidity creates ideal conditions for insects like termites and wood borers to thrive. Fabric damage: Moisture can cause mold and mildew to grow on upholstery, impacting both aesthetics and hygiene. Fading and discoloration: The intense sunlight in Surat can lead to furniture fading and discoloration over time.

These issues not only compromise the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but can also lead to costly repairs and even safety hazards.

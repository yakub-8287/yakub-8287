Common Furniture Issues in Surat: A Problem Statement

Many homes in Surat face unique challenges related to furniture. The city's humid climate can lead to wood warping and upholstery damage, while fluctuating temperatures cause materials to expand and contract, leading to cracks and instability. Improper furniture placement can also exacerbate these issues, potentially damaging valuable pieces. Ignoring these problems can lead to costly repairs down the line, impacting both your budget and the safety of your family. Furthermore, poorly placed furniture can create tripping hazards, especially in homes with children or elderly residents. The beautiful, intricate woodwork often found in traditional Gujarati homes requires special care and consideration during placement.

Common Furniture Problems in Surat

The humid climate of Surat, especially around Kosamba, presents unique challenges for furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and warping: High humidity weakens wood, leading to rot, warping, and structural damage. Pest infestation: Warm, humid conditions create ideal breeding grounds for termites and other wood-boring insects. Upholstery damage: Moisture can cause mold and mildew to develop in upholstery, affecting both its appearance and longevity. Loose joints and structural instability: Over time, furniture joints can loosen, affecting stability and safety.

These problems not only impact the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but can also lead to significant repair costs and even safety hazards. Ignoring these issues can quickly escalate the damage and expense.

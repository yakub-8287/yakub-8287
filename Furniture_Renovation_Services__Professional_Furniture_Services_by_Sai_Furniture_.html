Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's climate, with its fluctuating humidity and temperatures, presents unique challenges for furniture. Wooden pieces are particularly vulnerable to warping, cracking, and pest infestation. Upholstery can fade, tear, or become stained. These issues not only detract from the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but can also pose safety hazards. Ignoring these problems can lead to costly replacements down the line. For example, a seemingly minor crack in a wooden table leg can escalate into significant structural damage.

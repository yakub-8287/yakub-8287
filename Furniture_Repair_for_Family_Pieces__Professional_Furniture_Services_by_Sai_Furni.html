Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's climate, characterized by high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, presents unique challenges for furniture. Common problems we encounter include:

Wood rot and warping: The humid conditions can cause wood to swell, crack, and even rot. Loose joints and structural damage: Years of use can weaken joints and cause structural instability. Upholstery damage: Sun exposure and humidity can fade fabrics and cause tearing. Pest infestation: Termites and other insects are a common threat to wooden furniture.

Ignoring these issues can lead to costly repairs down the line, and in some cases, irreversible damage. Furthermore, damaged furniture can pose safety risks, especially if structural integrity is compromised.

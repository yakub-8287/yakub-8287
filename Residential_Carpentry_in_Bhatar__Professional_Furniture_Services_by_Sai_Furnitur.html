Common Furniture Problems in Surat & Their Solutions

Surat's humid climate and fluctuating temperatures can significantly affect your furniture's condition. Common problems include:

Wood rot and decay: Humidity can cause wood to swell, warp, and eventually rot, damaging the structural integrity of your furniture. Pest infestations: Warm, humid conditions can attract termites and other wood-boring insects. Cracks and splits: Changes in temperature and humidity can cause wood to expand and contract, leading to cracks and splits in your furniture. Loose joints and failing hardware: Over time, the glue and hardware holding your furniture together can weaken, requiring repairs.

These issues not only affect the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but can also pose safety risks. Addressing them promptly is crucial to avoid costly replacements and ensure the safety of your family.

The Problem: Furniture Distress in Surat's Climate

Surat's climate, characterized by high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, poses significant challenges to furniture. Wood can warp, crack, and become infested with pests. Common problems include:

Damaged joints: Loose joints and broken legs are prevalent, often due to shifting humidity levels. Wood rot and decay: Exposure to moisture can lead to significant deterioration in wooden furniture. Pest infestations: Termites and wood-boring insects are common in the region, requiring professional intervention. Upholstery damage: Sun exposure and wear and tear can quickly affect the quality of upholstery.

These issues can lead to costly repairs or even complete replacement, making professional maintenance crucial. Ignoring these problems poses safety risks and compromises the aesthetic appeal of your home or business.

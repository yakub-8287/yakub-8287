Common Furniture Problems in Surat: The Challenges of Our Climate

Surat's hot and humid climate presents unique challenges for your furniture. High humidity can lead to warping, cracking, and mold growth in wooden furniture. Temperature fluctuations can cause expansion and contraction, impacting the structural integrity of your pieces. This can lead to costly repairs or even complete replacement. Furthermore, improper repairs or use of low-quality materials can exacerbate these problems, leading to further damage and safety concerns. For example, a poorly repaired chair may become unstable and pose a risk of injury.

The Problem: Surat's Furniture Challenges

Many homes in Surat face common furniture issues. The hot and humid climate of Gujarat can cause warping, cracking, and insect infestation in wooden furniture. Improper storage or handling can lead to further damage. The cost of replacing antique or handcrafted pieces can be prohibitive, while attempting DIY repairs often results in more extensive damage. Furthermore, damaged furniture can pose safety risks, particularly if it's structurally compromised. For instance, a weakened chair leg in your home near the Tapi River could lead to accidents.

Common Furniture Issues in Surat's Climate

Surat's climate, characterized by high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, presents unique challenges for furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and warping: The high humidity can cause wooden furniture to absorb excess moisture, leading to rot, warping, and structural instability. Mildew and fungal growth: Damp conditions create ideal environments for mildew and fungal growth, damaging both the aesthetics and structural integrity of your furniture. Fabric deterioration: Humidity and temperature variations can cause fabric upholstery to fade, become brittle, and lose its resilience. Pest infestation: Certain insects thrive in humid environments, infesting wooden furniture and causing significant damage.

These problems not only affect the appearance of your furniture but can also compromise its safety and longevity, leading to costly replacements.

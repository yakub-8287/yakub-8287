Common Furniture Problems in Surat: The Challenges of Climate & Time

Surat's humid climate and fluctuating temperatures can significantly impact your furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and warping: High humidity can cause wood to swell, crack, and even rot. Loose joints and broken pieces: Years of wear and tear can weaken furniture structures. Damaged upholstery and fabric: Sun exposure and daily use can fade and tear fabrics. Pest infestation: Wood-boring insects thrive in humid environments.

Ignoring these problems can lead to costly replacements, potential safety hazards, and the loss of sentimental pieces. Proper furniture repair can prevent such issues.

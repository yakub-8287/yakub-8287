Common Furniture Problems in Surat

The unique climate of Surat, with its high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, presents several challenges for furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and warping: The humid weather can cause wood to swell, crack, and rot, especially in older, untreated pieces. Pest infestation: Termites and other wood-boring insects thrive in humid conditions, damaging furniture structures. Loose joints and broken components: Constant use and the effects of humidity can weaken furniture, leading to loose joints and broken pieces. Faded or damaged finishes: Exposure to sunlight and moisture can fade finishes and cause damage to the overall appearance.

These issues can not only affect the aesthetics of your furniture but can also pose safety risks. Ignoring these problems can lead to costly repairs down the line.

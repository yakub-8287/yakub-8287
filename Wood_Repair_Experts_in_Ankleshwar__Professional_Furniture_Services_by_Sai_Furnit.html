Common Furniture Problems in Surat: Recognizing the Need for Repair

The humid Surat climate, often characterized by fluctuating temperatures, takes a toll on wooden furniture. Common issues include:

Wood rot and decay: Prolonged exposure to moisture can lead to significant damage. Cracks and splits: Changes in temperature and humidity cause wood to expand and contract, resulting in unsightly cracks. Scratches and gouges: Daily wear and tear can leave your furniture looking worn. Loose joints and broken pieces: Over time, joints can loosen, leading to instability and potential safety hazards.

Ignoring these issues can lead to further damage, increasing repair costs and potentially compromising the structural integrity of your furniture. Ignoring the problem also carries safety risks for your family.

Common Furniture Problems in Surat

Surat's climate, with its fluctuating humidity and temperatures, poses significant challenges for furniture. Wood can warp, crack, and become susceptible to pest infestation. Ignoring these problems can lead to costly repairs or even the need for complete furniture replacement. Furthermore, damaged furniture can pose safety hazards, especially for children.

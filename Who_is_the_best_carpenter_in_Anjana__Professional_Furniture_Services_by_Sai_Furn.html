The Furniture Problems Facing Surat Residents

Surat's unique climate, with its high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, presents several challenges for furniture. Common problems include:

Wood rot and warping: The humid climate can cause wood to absorb moisture, leading to warping, cracking, and even rot. Pest infestations: Warm, humid conditions create ideal breeding grounds for termites and other wood-boring insects. Fabric damage: Excessive humidity can cause fabric upholstery to mildew or become stained. Loose joints and broken pieces: Years of use can cause furniture joints to loosen or components to break.

These issues not only affect the aesthetic appeal of your furniture but can also pose safety risks. Ignoring minor repairs can lead to more significant and costly damage down the line.

The Challenges of Furniture Repair in Surat

Surat's climate, characterized by high humidity and fluctuating temperatures, presents unique challenges to furniture. Common problems include:

Wood rot and warping: The humid weather can cause wood to expand and contract, leading to cracks, warping, and even rot, especially in older furniture pieces.
